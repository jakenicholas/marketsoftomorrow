Catch up on the highlights from this past weekend at Rolling Loud Miami 2024

Rolling Loud Miami 2024 marked a significant milestone, celebrating its 10th anniversary with a star-studded lineup and unforgettable...

By Jake Nicholas December 17, 2024

Rolling Loud Miami 2024 marked a significant milestone, celebrating its 10th anniversary with a star-studded lineup and unforgettable performances at Hard Rock Stadium from December 13 to 15. The festival featured headliners Future, Travis Scott, and Playboi Carti, each delivering electrifying sets that left attendees buzzing.

Day 1: Future's Fiery Performance

Future ignited the festival's energy right from the start with a dynamic set that had the crowd buzzing. Performing chart-topping hits like "March Madness," "Low Life," and "WAIT FOR U," he transformed the stage into a spectacle of fiery visuals, complete with a blazing building backdrop. The electrifying scene not only kept ravers warm but amplified the atmosphere, setting the tone for an unforgettable weekend.

Day 2: Travis Scott's Theatrical Set with Owen Wilson

Travis Scott's headlining performance was a highlight of the weekend. He made a grand entrance via helicopter, setting the tone for a high-energy show. The set reached a peak when actor Owen Wilson joined Scott on stage to perform "FE!N" from Scott's 2023 album, Utopia. Wilson's surprise appearance added a unique twist to the evening, especially considering his upcoming role in a film inspired by the Rolling Loud festival. 

Day 3: Playboi Carti's New Music Debut

Playboi Carti brought the festival to an exhilarating close, debuting tracks from his highly anticipated album, I AM MUSIC. His high-energy performance, paired with a dazzling fireworks display, created a mesmerizing atmosphere that lit up the night and delivered a powerful finale to an unforgettable weekend of music and celebration.

Special Guest Appearances and Notable Moments

The festival was packed with thrilling surprise guest appearances that kept fans buzzing:

  • Kodak Black's Set: Kodak Black captured attention with his performance, though fans were divided, voicing concerns about his stage presence and punctuality.

  • Don Toliver's Performance: Don Toliver turned up the excitement by bringing out Offset for a lively, crowd-led birthday celebration, adding a unique and festive touch to his set.

  • YG MARLEY and Lauryn Hill: In one of the festival's most unforgettable moments, YG MARLEY surprised the crowd by inviting his mother, the iconic Lauryn Hill, to join him on stage, creating a heartfelt and powerful highlight that resonated with the audience.

Festival Experience and Activations

The festival’s immersive activations added an extra layer of excitement and personalization for attendees:

  • Bunny’s Bae Bar: Festival-goers enjoyed complimentary glam services, making it a popular stop for those looking to refresh their style.

  • Loud Cutz: Guests lined up for free haircuts, blending fresh looks with festival vibes.

  • Tattoo Parlour: For those seeking a permanent memory, the tattoo parlor offered unique Panda-designed tattoos, allowing fans to commemorate their experience in a bold and creative way.

Rolling Loud Miami 2024 marked its 10th anniversary with a standout celebration of hip-hop, delivering unforgettable performances, surprise guest appearances, and immersive activities that reinforced its position as one of the genre’s most iconic festivals.
