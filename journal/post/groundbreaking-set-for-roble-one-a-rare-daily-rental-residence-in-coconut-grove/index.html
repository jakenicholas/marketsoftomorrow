Groundbreaking set for Roble One, a rare daily-rental residence in Coconut Grove

Coconut Grove is about to welcome one of its most unique new developments— Roble One , a boutique 16-residence condo that blends modern...

By Jake Nicholas June 3, 2025

Coconut Grove is about to welcome one of its most unique new developments—Roble One, a boutique 16-residence condo that blends modern design with short-term rental flexibility in a neighborhood known for charm, culture, and walkability. Breaking ground on June 5th at 3069 Plaza Street, just steps from Coral Gables, Roble One stands out as one of the only new-construction projects in the Grove fully zoned for daily rentals—unlocking rare investment potential in a tightly held market.

Developed by SSA Group, a global real estate firm with over 30 projects across three continents, the team is finalizing a $7.87 million construction loan with Intercredit Bank to kick off vertical development. Completion is expected in 2026.

What makes Roble One a standout is its built-in flexibility. Instead of following the conventional path of building two townhomes on the site, the developers secured approval for 16 short-term rentable units—a strategic move that offers owners both lifestyle perks and income upside. It’s a modern approach tailored to today’s hybrid buyer.

Designed by Atelier 305 and constructed by BEC Group Services, Roble One blends clean, contemporary architecture with an intimate footprint. Two-bedroom units start at $780,000, with sales and marketing led by The Opes Group at Compass, and branding and digital marketing managed by creative agency Formula.

Located across from Miami’s soon-to-debut 10-mile linear park and within five minutes of both Miracle Mile and Grove Marina, Roble One puts residents at the intersection of greenery, city life, and waterfront access—making it a compelling addition to the evolving West Grove landscape.

For more information, visit Roble One Residences or contact sales@robleoneresidences.com
