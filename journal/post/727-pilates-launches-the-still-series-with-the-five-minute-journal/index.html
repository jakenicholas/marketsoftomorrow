727 Pilates launches The Still Series with The Five Minute Journal

Founded by Miami-based entrepreneur and interior designer Arantxa Salazar, the studio now enters its next chapter with an expanded lineup of signature classes built around movement, recovery, and ritual.

By Jake Nicholas January 16, 2026

727 Pilates Studio is redefining what a wellness studio can be in Miami’s Little River. Rooted in the angel number 727—symbolizing reflection and inner alignment—the design-forward sanctuary blends Pilates, sculpt, meditation, and contrast therapy into a single, intentional experience. Founded by Miami-based entrepreneur and interior designer Arantxa Salazar, the studio opened in late spring 2025 and now enters its next chapter with an expanded lineup of signature classes built around movement, recovery, and ritual.

Designed by Atelier 7, the 1,875-square-foot space feels closer to a members-only retreat than a traditional studio. Warm, moody lighting, custom scents, and a palette of maroon, taupe, and gold create an atmosphere that’s grounding from the moment you step inside. The layout is intentionally intimate: a main reformer room with 10 machines anchored by chandeliers and a runner rug, a dedicated mat studio with 13 spaces for small-group sessions (heated and non-heated), and a Recovery Sanctuary featuring an infrared sauna, cold plunge, and tea station for post-class reset.

“727 was created from my own journey toward balance,” Salazar shares. “I wanted to design a space that feels grounding and intentional—a place that invites people to strengthen, recover, and reconnect through both movement and stillness.”

At its core, 727 is where strength meets stillness. The studio’s curated programming blends Reformer Pilates, Mat Pilates, Sculpt, and Guided Meditation into cohesive rituals that move the body while calming the mind. The Reformer Series ranges from ROOT, a beginner-friendly foundation class, to FLOW STATE, ALIGN SCULPT, and FUSION SCULPT—dynamic, strength-driven sessions that incorporate the studio’s signature Wunda Chair. ELEVATE, a chair-only class, focuses on precision and breath-led sculpting.

The Mat Series includes SCULPT, a controlled full-body flow, and IGNITE, a heated mat class that intensifies each sequence with infrared warmth. RESTORE combines yin, breathwork, and meditation for mobility and emotional balance, while DUALITY offers a guided contrast therapy ritual pairing sauna and cold plunge. Every class is led by a highly trained team committed to conscious movement and thoughtful pacing.

To mark this new chapter and the start of a new year, 727 partnered with Intelligent Change - the global wellness brand behind The Five Minute Journal - to host The Still Series, an intimate evening experience designed to slow everything down and come center with abundance and affirmations. Each event includes a guided meditation and sound healing session on the mat, followed by intentional journaling. Guests receive a gifted Five Minute Journal to take home.

With flexible memberships, curated class packs, and access to its Recovery Sanctuary, 727 meets guests wherever they are in their wellness journey. With exciting pop-ups, collaborations, and seasonal experiences, the studio positions itself as one of Miami’s most intentional destinations for movement and restoration.
