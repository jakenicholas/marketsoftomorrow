The Palm Beaches

Unique loft residences in the heart of downtown West Palm Beach

Haven Palm Beach has just introduced a limited collection of exquisite waterfront residences, where modern luxury meets unmatched...

By Jake Nicholas September 6, 2024

Haven Palm Beach has just introduced a limited collection of exquisite waterfront residences, where modern luxury meets unmatched comfort. Nestled in the heart of downtown West Palm Beach, just steps from the vibrant energy of Palm Beach Island, these final six homes offer an unparalleled lifestyle.

Chic restaurants, world-class galleries, and high-end boutiques are all within easy reach, right at your doorstep.

Haven Palm Beach showcases a curated selection of meticulously designed residences, each promising an exceptional living experience.

Waterfront Zen (4 Bed / 4.5 Bath, $5,400,000)

A rare oasis directly on the park, this chic 4-bedroom residence boasts unobstructed water views from every room and features 3 private balconies. Indulge in the spa-like ambiance with a custom-built 14-foot water feature, 17-foot high ceilings, and expansive walk-in closets.

Yacht Club (3 Bed / 3.5 Bath, $2,950,000)

Modern luxury awaits at the Yacht Club residence. This chic 3-bedroom loft boasts breathtaking views of the marina and yachts, complete with 16-foot high ceilings and a custom-built bar area.

Palm Park (3 Bed / 3.5 Bath, $2,950,000)

Unwind in unparalleled luxury at the Palm Park residence. This spacious loft offers unobstructed water views and features high-end finishes throughout.

Haven Palm Beach also offers a variety of 1 Bedroom / 1.5 Bathrooms Pied-A-Terre options, including:

  • Cloud Nine (Price Upon Request)

  • Sunrise (Price Upon Request)

  • Palm Breeze (Price Upon Request)

Unparalleled Amenities

  • Exclusive Memberships: Enjoy immediate access to a fleet of boats and jet skis through the JetRide membership program, located just steps from your residence.

  • Friends of the Ben: Maintain your well-being with a complimentary membership to The Ben Autograph Hotel’s fitness center, and join their offerings in a variety of classes.

Become part of a vibrant community that cherishes art, wellness, and culinary experiences. Haven Palm Beach residences offer more than just a place to live; they offer a way of life.
