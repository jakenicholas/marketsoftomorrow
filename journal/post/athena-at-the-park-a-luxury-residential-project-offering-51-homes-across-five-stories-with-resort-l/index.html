Athena at the Park, a luxury residential project offering 51 homes across five stories with resort-like amenities is coming to Nashville's west end by the Fall of 2024

By Jake Nicholas March 4, 2024

Slated for its grand opening in Fall 2024, Athena at the Park is poised to redefine luxurious living in Nashville's West End. This exclusive residential building, featuring 51 units spread over five floors, promises an array of high-end amenities designed to blend resort-style living with the convenience of urban life. Residents can look forward to enjoying a variety of indulgences such as a swimming pool with private cabanas, a state-of-the-art fitness center and sauna, community bikes for exploring the neighborhood, and a rooftop sky lounge that offers breathtaking views.

Each spacious unit is meticulously designed to enhance outdoor living, boasting terraces and balconies alongside luxury features including sleek paneled appliances, elegant hardwood floors, zero-entry showers, and innovative smart mirrors. The architectural vision, led by Jesse T. Wilmoth and his team, is to cultivate a sense of community within this classic neighborhood that enjoys unparalleled views of downtown, courtesy of its close proximity to the lush greenery of Centennial Park.

Athena at the Park, developed by Richland Building Partners, represents a significant step forward in luxury condominium living in Nashville's vibrant West-End neighborhood. Located at 321 31st Avenue North, this development is not only about providing top-tier finishes and unique wellness-focused amenities but also situating residents in an enviable location that combines the tranquility of park-side living with the dynamism of downtown Nashville.

Chris Barnhizer, the founder of Richland Building Partners, emphasizes the importance of exceptional spaces in extraordinary locations. Athena’s proximity to Centennial Park, various greenways, a dog park, and the broader Nashville area highlights the development's prime location for those seeking a balanced, upscale lifestyle.

An immersive sales event at the Parthenon underscored Athena's connection to its namesake and the importance of heritage and culture in this development. Smith Gee Studio’s modern architectural design prioritizes open floor plans, expansive windows, and private rooftop decks for top-floor residences, ensuring that each unit offers a unique outdoor living experience complemented by high-end appliances and finishes from brands like Sub Zero-Wolf and Lux View Windows.

The development doesn’t skimp on amenities, offering residents 24-hour concierge service, dedicated parking, a luxurious pool and sauna, fitness center, clubhouse, and rooftop deck, among other features. Athena also partners with various amenity providers to offer convenient, healthy lifestyle options right on the premises, including The Agora Market and fitness and poolside services.

Barnhizer highlights the development’s focus on exceeding expectations through meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to fostering community. With 14 of the 51 condos already presold, Athena at the Park is well on its way to becoming one of Nashville’s most sought-after living spaces. For those interested in this unparalleled living experience, feel free to flip through their brochure to learn more.
