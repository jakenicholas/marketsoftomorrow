The Florida Keys

Three Waters Resort & Marina, a Tribute Portfolio Resort, to open in fall 2024, the newest addition and crown jewel to the dynamic Islamorada Resort Collection

By Jake Nicholas May 21, 2024

Slated for a Fall 2024 opening, Three Waters Resort & Marina will be a new jewel in the Islamorada Resort Collection, which includes the well-loved Amara Cay Resort and La Siesta Resort & Villas. This upscale Tribute Portfolio Resort in the Florida Keys will stretch across 15 acres of prime oceanfront land and offer 214 elegantly designed island-inspired guest rooms. Alongside expansive gathering and event spaces, the resort is set to launch nine distinctive culinary venues, adding a vibrant new dining scene to the "Sport Fishing Capital of the World."

Nestled in a unique location that embodies the essence of the sun, sand, and sea, Three Waters Resort & Marina aims to provide a plethora of sophisticated and genuine experiences for its guests. These include an adult-centric resort-within-a-resort called The Cove, featuring king-bedded rooms and suites, a private beach lagoon, and a wellness-focused Wave Motion Studio. The broader resort will cater to families and other guests with its Florida Keys-inspired décor and a host of amenities designed to enhance the visitor experience.

Educational and wellness programming will emphasize the maritime and ecological uniqueness of the area, with offerings such as boating and fishing lessons, fly casting, and more, supported by experienced local captains and fishermen. The marina at Three Waters boasts the deepest channel in the Upper Keys and includes facilities like a new boat valet service and a full fleet of fishing vessels, ensuring a premium on-water experience for boating enthusiasts.

Culinary offerings will be a cornerstone of the Three Waters experience, with venues ranging from a signature restaurant featuring live-fire cooking to a tiki bar, a street-food-inspired marketplace, and upscale bars and cafes. Each dining establishment will highlight the best local ingredients and offer spaces for both lively social gatherings and intimate, serene dining.

As a newly added member of the Marriott Bonvoy Tribute Portfolio, guests at Three Waters Resort & Marina will also enjoy the benefits of earning and redeeming points during their stays.

For more detailed information and reservations, visitors can check the resort’s website at www.threewatersresort.com and follow their updates on social media at @ThreeWatersResort
