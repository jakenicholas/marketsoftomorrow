The Nora Hotel to welcome Pastis, an iconic New York City Parisian-style brasserie in West Palm Beach's Nora District

The Nora District , West Palm Beach’s vibrant, pedestrian-friendly destination announced today the addition of Pastis, the quintessential...

By Jake Nicholas August 20, 2024

The Nora District, West Palm Beach’s vibrant, pedestrian-friendly destination announced today the addition of Pastis, the quintessential New York City Parisian-style brasserie by James Beard Award-winning restaurateur Stephen Starr (STARR Restaurants), as The Nora Hotel’s ground floor restaurant. In addition to Pastis, STARR Restaurants will co-create the hotel’s rooftop restaurant and lounge as well as operate in-room dining service. This will mark the fourth location for Pastis, with existing restaurants in Manhattan’s Meatpacking District, Washington D.C. and Miami.

Image Courtesy: Pastis, Joshua Perez

The Nora Hotel will be a 201-key boutique hotel developed in partnership with BD Hotels and Sean MacPherson. BD Hotels is notably behind some of New York City’s most iconic hotels including The Greenwich, The Bowery, The Mercer, Hotel Chelsea and others. The Nora Hotel marks BD Hotels second hotel collaboration with Wheelock Street Capital, after the successful relaunch of Hotel Chelsea in 2023.

“At its core, Nora is a district rooted in hospitality. The Nora Hotel will serve as a focal point of the district, a place where guests can experience world-class hospitality paired with impressive food and beverage operators, among other lifestyle and wellness-driven tenants just steps away from the hotel. We look forward to welcoming Pastis as one of the greatest neighborhood bistros in the country to anchor what we believe will be the premier hotel offering in West Palm Beach,” said Richard Born, owner of BD Hotels.

Images Courtesy: Pastis, Joshua Perez

Pastis West Palm Beach will mark the second location for the concept in South Florida. The critically acclaimed restaurant has anchored Manhattan’s Meatpacking District for nearly 15 years, expanding into Miami’s Wynwood Arts District in May 2023 and Washington D.C. in January 2024. 

“Since opening Pastis in Miami last year, the positive response to the restaurant has been astounding. Residents of Palm Beach, West Palm Beach and the surrounding areas have asked me countless times to bring Pastis to their neighborhood. With the development of Nora, one of the most exciting and ambitious projects to hit South Florida, we are now going to make that a reality. STARR Restaurants is proud to work with Richard Born and his team to be a part of this incredible project,” said Stephen Starr, founder and CEO of STARR Restaurants. 

Image Courtesy: Pastis, Joshua Perez

Pastis West Palm Beach will encompass 13,300 square feet of The Nora Hotel’s ground floor including both indoor and outdoor spaces. The restaurant, which is set to transport guests to a classic Parisian brasserie, will feature the brand’s iconic curved zinc bar, white subway tiles and signature red banquettes. Guests can expect Pastis’ classic hors d’oeuvres such as French Onion Soup, Escargots, Tuna Carpaccio, and Warm Shrimp Salad. A selection of traditional Steak Frites will be available with different cuts of beef, along with entrées such as Moules Frites, Duck Confit, Chicken Paillard and Grilled Branzino. Cocktails will feature an array of options like the St. Tropez Spritz, Lady Marmalade and a mini martini called Le Petit Pickle. 

Developed by NDT Development, Place Projects and Wheelock Street Capital, Nora will be a dynamic district that breathes new life into Downtown West Palm Beach, becoming the ultimate amenity for the greater Palm Beach County community.

Phase I of Nora, which will include over 150,000 square feet of adaptive reuse and new development, broke ground in June 2023 and is slated for completion in early 2025. Turn-of-the-century railway warehouses will be reimagined into ground-floor retail celebrating local and national talent with innovative open spaces and art in public places. Phase I will also include over 55,000 square feet of second-floor Class-A office space. 

As Downtown West Palm Beach rapidly evolves, Nora is set to become the epicenter of dining, arts and culture. Additional confirmed retailers include Loco Taqueria & Oyster Bar, H&H Bagels, Van Leeuwen Ice Cream, The Garret Group, Juliana’s Pizza, Celis Juice Bar and Café, Del Mar Mediterranean Restaurant, Sana Skin Studio, Mint, The Spot Barbershop, [solidcore] and Sunday Motor Co. with more to be announced. 

Nora’s comprehensive master plan is led by the City of West Palm Beach and nationally acclaimed urban planning firm PlusUrbia. For more information about Nora, visit norawpb.com and follow @norawestpalm

Nora is currently leasing 55,000 square feet of Class-A office for creatives, innovators and dynamic businesses at the heart of the district. The office features expansive district views, customizable layouts and spec suites with high-end finishes. Office tenants will have best-in-class food and beverage establishments, boutique fitness and wellness, and lifestyle retail offerings just steps away. For information regarding leasing, contact Francis X. Scire, Head of Leasing at fxs@norawpb.com.
