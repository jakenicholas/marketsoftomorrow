Dependable Equities debut Ombelle Fort Lauderdale turn-key residences

Dependable Equities proudly unveils Ombelle Fort Lauderdale, an upscale residential project located at 300 NE 3rd Avenue in Flagler...

By Jake Nicholas October 30, 2024

Dependable Equities proudly unveils Ombelle Fort Lauderdale, an upscale residential project located at 300 NE 3rd Avenue in Flagler Village. Rising 43 stories, Ombelle offers 775 ready-to-live-in residences, over 11,000 square feet of retail space on the ground floor, and more than 100,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor amenities. This prime location situates residents at the heart of Downtown Fort Lauderdale’s dynamic dining, shopping, and arts scene.

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

Isaac Schlesinger, Principal at Dependable Equities, emphasizes the development’s unique approach:

"Ombelle Fort Lauderdale’s residences and extensive amenities foster a holistic, active lifestyle beyond conventional boundaries. The high-end finishes offered here come at prices that redefine value in South Florida’s real estate market.”

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

Dependable Equities, co-founded by Schlesinger and Simon Dushinsky of the Rabsky Group, brings a wealth of experience with thousands of luxury residences developed in New York. Currently, the firm manages over 2 million square feet of active projects and a real estate portfolio worth more than $4 billion. This commitment to quality and value underscores their expansion into the Fort Lauderdale market, providing buyers with accessibly priced luxury residences.

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

“Ombelle follows Rabsky Group's proven development model that has delivered over 4 million square feet of residential space in New York,” explains Dushinsky. "By leveraging our in-house construction team, we offer a premium product with enhanced value, such as floor-to-ceiling tiled master bathrooms—an exclusive result of our team’s expertise.”

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

Designed by the acclaimed ODA team, Ombelle’s residences range from studios to three-bedroom units, sized between 465 and 1,400 square feet, along with opulent penthouses up to 2,600 square feet. Each residence incorporates the latest in smart home technology, premium appliances, and custom Italian-designed kitchens and bathrooms. The open-concept interiors feature 10-foot ceilings, elegant finishes, and a selection of designer-curated furnishings, giving residents the option of a fully furnished living space.

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

The flexible rental policy, allowing leases of at least 30 days, provides owners with versatility while maintaining a high standard of living. Eran Chen AIA, ODA’s founder, notes, “Ombelle integrates Fort Lauderdale’s urban and natural beauty, inspiring a sense of community and connection to the city’s waterways and green spaces.”

The grand entrance features a 15,000-square-foot lobby and cultural garden, creating an inviting atmosphere for residents and guests alike. Inside, residents have exclusive access to wellness, social, and recreational spaces meticulously curated by Arch Amenities. Highlights include a wellness floor with yoga and Pilates studios, saunas, Zen gardens, and a comprehensive fitness center. Other amenities feature coworking spaces, a chef’s kitchen, music and art studios, pickleball courts, and resort-style pools.

Image Credit: Ombelle Residences Fort Lauderdale

Jay Phillip Parker, CEO of Douglas Elliman Florida Region, remarks, “Fort Lauderdale is the ultimate combination of urban sophistication and coastal charm. Ombelle’s remarkable amenities and design make it a standout addition to Downtown Fort Lauderdale, meeting the modern lifestyle demands of this vibrant city.”

Douglas Elliman Development Marketing exclusively handles sales for Ombelle Fort Lauderdale, with units starting in the $400,000 range. For a private viewing, visit the Presentation Gallery at 315 NE 3rd Ave, Suite CU103, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33301, or explore ownombelle.com
