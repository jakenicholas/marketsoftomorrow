Inside Look: The amenities at Tampa's NOVEL Beach Park

By Jake Nicholas July 16, 2024

The highly anticipated grand opening of NOVEL Beach Park has been nothing short of a success. With over 50% of all apartment homes already being leased, NOVEL Beach Park continues to fill up quickly, offering residents an unparalleled amenity list.

Beyond the lavish interiors, NOVEL Beach Park is a lifestyle destination in its own right. Residents can indulge in the serene waterside pool and lounge, complete with outdoor kitchens and an infinity-edge saltwater pool, or unwind in the 9th floor Sunset Lounge with panoramic views of Old Tampa Bay. For those seeking to stay active, the fitness club features state-of-the-art Technogym equipment, private yoga studios, Peloton bikes, and complimentary aromatherapy cooling towels.

Another amazing offering is NOVEL's Gear Lounge - allowing residents to complimentary rent stand-up paddle boards, bikes, coolers, pickleball and tennis sets, yoga mats, speakers, kayaks, tools, and more - all a part of the resident experience.

Pet lovers will appreciate the pet-friendly amenities, including a pet spa and outdoor park with covered seating and double-gated entry. Meanwhile, the co-working lounge offers a variety of spaces to work from home, including communal booths and a large conference room with screen-sharing capabilities.

The homes feature smart home technology, spacious balconies, and luxury plank flooring. The kitchens are a chef's dream, equipped with quartz countertops, wood shaker cabinetry, and Samsung ENERGY STAR® stainless steel appliances. Additionally, the bathrooms offer a spa-like retreat with large-format porcelain tile surrounds and frameless LED backlit mirrors.

Conveniently located near a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options, NOVEL Beach Park offers residents easy access to everything they need and more. From upscale restaurants like Elevage SoHo Kitchen & Bar to popular shopping destinations like International Plaza and Bay Street, there's something for everyone just steps away from your doorstep.

As NOVEL Beach Park continues to welcome its first residents, this waterfront oasis shows why it’s more than just renting an apartment— residents subscribe to a vibrant community where luxury living meets coastal charm.
