Coral Isles nears sellout as it reaches its final phase

Coral Isles by Kenco Communities is now celebrating closeout, marking the final opportunity to own within this exclusive Palm Beach Gardens address.

By Jake Nicholas March 3, 2026

Coral Isles by Kenco Communities has officially entered closeout at Avenir, marking the last opportunity to secure a luxury estate home within one of Palm Beach Gardens’ most established and fast-growing master-planned communities.

Only a limited collection of residences, select homesites, and professionally furnished model homes are available, signaling the final chapter for one of Avenir’s most sought-after neighborhoods. Buyers now have the choice between immediate-occupancy estate homes, custom homesites for personalized builds, and designer-curated model residences available for purchase/leaseback—each reflecting Kenco Communities’ signature craftsmanship, architectural detail, and long-standing reputation across Palm Beach County.

For a limited time, Kenco Communities is offering buyers up to $250,000 in savings on select move‑in‑ready homes — a great opportunity to move into a luxury residence without the wait. These stunning Palm Beach homes are ready now, so buyers can move right into the lifestyle they’ve been dreaming of before this sought‑after community sells out.

Developed by family-owned Kenco Communities, Coral Isles continues a legacy spanning more than three decades and thousands of homes across South Florida’s premier communities, including Mirasol, BallenIsles, and Addison Reserve. At Coral Isles, estate residences range from approximately 3,200 to more than 6,000 AC square feet, offering expansive floorplans, soaring ceilings, dramatic natural light, chef-inspired kitchens, spa-style primary suites, and seamless indoor-outdoor living designed for modern Florida lifestyles. Homesites average generous quarter-acre lots, allowing space for private pools, outdoor kitchens, and fully customized outdoor environments.

Discover two exceptional move‑in‑ready estate homes now available at Coral Isles—each offering luxury, space, and style designed for resort‑style living in Palm Beach County.

The Cozumel IV: This single‑level, 4‑bedroom, 4.5‑bath estate spans over 4,300 square feet of beautifully designed living space. The open layout flows from a chef’s kitchen with quartz countertops to a great room with soaring ceilings. The primary suite offers a spa‑inspired retreat, while sliding glass walls open to a covered patio and pool for effortless indoor‑outdoor living. Priced at $2,594,472.

The Dominica Grande: Bold and timeless, this two‑story, master‑down residence features 5 bedrooms, 6 full and 2 half baths across more than 6,000 square feet. A grand foyer and sweeping staircase make a lasting impression, complemented by a gourmet kitchen, spacious loft, and private club room. Outdoors, a resort‑style pool and summer kitchen complete this South Florida retreat. Priced at $3,172,687.

Beyond the homes, Coral Isles residents are fully integrated into the broader Avenir master plan—one of Palm Beach Gardens’ largest and most forward-looking communities. Spanning thousands of acres, Avenir blends luxury living with nature, anchored by a vast preserve, miles of walking and biking trails, expansive green space, and resort-style amenities including clubhouses, pools, fitness facilities, and racquet sports. The community’s walkable, golf-cart-friendly layout encourages connection while maintaining privacy and tranquility within each neighborhood.

A major next chapter for Avenir opening this Summer is the Town Center, a planned 200,000-square-foot retail and lifestyle hub designed as the social and commercial heart of the community. Offerings will include curated dining, boutique retail, wellness services, and everyday essentials such as Publix and Walgreens, further enhancing convenience and connectivity for residents. Complementing this lifestyle infrastructure are key on-site services, including a micro-hospital, medical offices, fire station, and charter school—reinforcing Avenir’s long-term vision as a fully integrated residential destination.

With construction advancing and closeout now underway, Coral Isles is nearing completion—transforming from a flagship neighborhood into a closeout opportunity. For buyers seeking a newly built luxury estate home within one of Palm Beach Gardens’ most comprehensive master-planned environments, this marks the final chance to enter Coral Isles before it sells out.
