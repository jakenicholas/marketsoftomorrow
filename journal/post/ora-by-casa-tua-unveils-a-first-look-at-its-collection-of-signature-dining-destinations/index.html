ORA by Casa Tua unveils a first look at its collection of signature dining destinations

A new chapter in refined living is coming to Brickell, as ORA by Casa Tua unveils its first interior renderings, offering a glimpse into...

By Jake Nicholas April 10, 2025

A new chapter in refined living is coming to Brickell, as ORA by Casa Tua unveils its first interior renderings, offering a glimpse into one of Miami’s most anticipated residential developments. Known for its elevated hospitality and timeless elegance, Casa Tua brings its signature style to ORA through a collaboration with the acclaimed design firm m2atelier.

Every detail at ORA has been meticulously curated to reflect a lifestyle of warmth, sophistication, and effortless luxury. The interiors blend natural materials with soft textures and sleek finishes, resulting in spaces that are both inviting and architecturally striking. Residences are thoughtfully designed to maximize light and views, with flowing layouts that connect indoor and outdoor living. Meanwhile, the amenity spaces foster a sense of connection—whether residents are seeking quiet relaxation or vibrant social energy, ORA delivers both in perfect harmony.

In true Casa Tua fashion, food is at the heart of the experience. ORA will feature a collection of distinctive dining concepts, each designed to reflect the global yet intimate spirit of the brand. Here’s a closer look at the four signature restaurants set to open within the development:

Uva

Wine lovers will feel at home in Uva, a refined enoteca where an extensive collection of sommelier-curated wines meets exceptional Mediterranean fare. Lush with character and charm, Uva invites you to slow down and savor each sip and bite. The wine list spans hundreds of labels from around the world, thoughtfully paired with elegant, seasonal dishes that celebrate the Mediterranean's coastal bounty—perfect for an indulgent dinner or an evening of discovery.

Fuoco

Fuoco offers a return to the beauty of simplicity. This intimate dining concept pays homage to timeless cooking methods, placing quality ingredients and heartfelt preparation at the forefront. With a warm, minimalist design and an atmosphere that encourages conversation, Fuoco evokes the feeling of a private dinner party—where every meal tells a story, and every detail is intentional.

Image: Terra, Fortune International Group & The Boundary

Vento

Floating high above the city, Vento is ORA’s rooftop omakase bar and lounge, where curated cocktails meet panoramic views of Brickell, Biscayne Bay, and Coconut Grove. Designed as a sleek yet breezy retreat, Vento is the perfect setting for sunset drinks, weekend poolside lounging, or late-night gatherings under the stars. With a menu of light bites and vibrant energy, it captures the rhythm and beauty of Miami from a striking new perspective.

Terra

Open 24 hours a day, Terra is more than just a market and bakery—it’s a lively gathering place where morning espresso rituals meet late-night cravings. The space is designed to feel alive at every hour, with fresh breads, artisanal pastries, and gourmet bites served in a casual yet elevated setting. Whether you're stopping in for a quick breakfast or catching up with friends over coffee, Terra’s upbeat ambiance makes it a neighborhood staple in the making.

​ORA by Casa Tua, an upcoming luxury residential development located in Miami's Brickell district, offers flexible ownership options for both short-term and long-term stays, featuring residences ranging from studios to four-bedroom units. The development embodies Casa Tua's signature Italian elegance, blending sophisticated design with natural elements to create warm and inviting living spaces.

Residents will have access to a world-class spa and fitness center, social lounges, and co-working spaces. Additionally, ORA will feature a sky garden at the center of the building, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding city and water. The project aims to foster a vibrant, globally inspired community, offering an oasis of art and culture curated by the iconic Casa Tua brand. ​
