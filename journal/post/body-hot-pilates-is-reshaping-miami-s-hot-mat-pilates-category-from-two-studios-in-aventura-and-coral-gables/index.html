BODY Hot Pilates is reshaping Miami's hot mat Pilates category from two studios in Aventura and Coral Gables

BODY Hot Pilates expands across Miami with two infrared studios in Aventura and Coral Gables, built on the brand's Hot Pilates, Hotter Results philosophy.

By Jake Nicholas June 19, 2026

Miami's wellness rotation has bent decisively toward high-heat Pilates over the past few years — infrared studios opening across South Florida, mat classes layered with sculpting and HIIT, and a handful of brands building serious waitlists among locals and seasonal residents alike. The category has a new leader. BODY Hot Pilates, the high-heat concept founded by former college athlete Lyndsey Meade, has expanded across Miami with two open studios in Aventura and Coral Gables — built on one tight brand philosophy: Hot Pilates, Hotter Results.

Classes run 50 minutes inside a studio outfitted with top-tier infrared heating, blending classical and contemporary Pilates principles with high-repetition movement. The heat itself does meaningful work in the room — increasing blood flow, unlocking deeper ranges of motion, releasing endorphins, and elevating cardiovascular health — while the instructor-led programming layers in the strength and form work that defines a serious Pilates session.

Every BODY instructor is licensed and certified through a 100-hour training program with ongoing BODY-format training designed to keep the experience consistent across every studio. Classes are kept intentionally small for hands-on guidance and tailored corrections — every session built closer to a private lesson than a packed group class.

The lineup spans the foundational Hot Pilates, Hot Pilates Sculpt for added resistance work, Hot HIIT Pilates for a higher cardiovascular load, and targeted formats including Arms & Abs and Abs & Buns. The result is a rotating weekly schedule that builds across formats rather than repeating the same class day after day.

Meade founded BODY on the conviction that hot Pilates done at a higher standard — small classes, licensed instructors, consistent format — produces results other studios can't match. The Hot Pilates, Hotter Results positioning has translated to consistently full class rosters and a strong returning-client base since the brand expanded into its current two-studio footprint.

With Aventura and Coral Gables both now open and spots regularly filling fast, BODY has emerged as one of Miami's most actively booked hot Pilates concepts — and a signal of where the city's high-end fitness rotation is heading next.
