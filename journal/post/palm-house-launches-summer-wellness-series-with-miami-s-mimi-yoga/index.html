Palm House launches summer wellness series with Miami’s Mimi Yoga

Unveiled just in time for the season, Palm House has announced a new summer wellness partnership with Mimi Yoga, one of Miami’s most...

By Jake Nicholas June 17, 2025

Unveiled just in time for the season, Palm House has announced a new summer wellness partnership with Mimi Yoga, one of Miami’s most in-demand studios. Beginning July 12, the boutique Palm Beach hotel will host six curated weekends of movement and mindfulness, featuring sunrise classes led by Mimi Ghandour and her team every Saturday and Sunday at 8:30 a.m. through August 17.

The series brings the energy of Mimi Yoga’s Wynwood and Coconut Grove studios to Palm Beach’s newest luxury address. Known for her signature vinyasa flows and inclusive approach to practice, Ghandour has built a global following through her community-first ethos and design-conscious studios. Her classes are open to all levels and will be held on custom Palm House-branded Bala mats, with special brand moments and wellness activations layered into the experience.

Classes are complimentary for hotel guests, while locals can join for $30 per session. As an added incentive for Miami followers, Palm House is offering 20% off weekend stays during the series using the promo code “MIMI.”

The collaboration marks a fresh chapter for the recently opened Palm House, the first U.S. outpost from London’s Iconic Luxury Hotels collection. Located just steps from the beach, the 79-room hotel blends whimsical British charm with Palm Beach sophistication. Guests can enjoy hourly Evian spritzes poolside, inventive Nikkei cuisine at The Dining Room, or cocktails at the luminous pink marble Palm Bar. A dedicated beach concierge and art-filled interiors—from local seashell installations to Slim Aarons photography—round out the hotel’s breezy, yet luxurious, atmosphere.

The Mimi Yoga partnership adds a new dimension to Palm House’s summer programming and reflects Iconic Luxury Hotels’ broader commitment to integrating wellness into each of its global destinations, from countryside retreats in the UK to coastal escapes like Palm Beach.

To book a Summer Wellness Series class, email info@palmhouse.com. To book a stay at Palm House during a Summer Wellness Series weekend, please visit www.palmhouse.com and use promo code “MIMI” for 20% off best available rates for exclusive summer rates.
