Eric Silagy joins Related Ross to accelerate South Florida’s transformation

Related Ross has appointed Eric Silagy as Vice Chairman, signaling a major step in the company’s ambitious effort to position West Palm...

By Jake Nicholas May 21, 2025

Related Ross has appointed Eric Silagy as Vice Chairman, signaling a major step in the company’s ambitious effort to position West Palm Beach and the broader South Florida region as a national epicenter for business, innovation, and civic investment.

In his new role, Silagy will focus on expanding public-private partnerships, spearheading economic development, and guiding long-term initiatives that support South Florida’s rapid transformation. The announcement comes as Related Ross advances a nearly $10 billion investment strategy across offices, residential, retail, hospitality, healthcare, education, and even golf—anchoring West Palm Beach as a model city for inclusive urban growth.

“The scale of transformation underway in West Palm Beach and South Florida demands leaders fluent in both the public and private spheres—individuals who can broker catalytic deals and build genuine trust across diverse communities,” said Stephen M. Ross, Founder, CEO, and Chairman of Related Ross. “Eric has demonstrated throughout his career an ability to unite business, government, and civic interests to deliver on ambitious goals. Bringing him on board underscores our intent to move boldly and collaboratively as we shape the next chapter of Florida.”

Silagy joins Related Ross following a two-decade career at Florida Power & Light Company (FPL), where he served as Chairman, President, and CEO. Under his leadership, FPL invested more than $60 billion in infrastructure, built the largest portfolio of utility-owned solar plants in the nation, and transformed Florida’s power grid into one of the most advanced and reliable in the U.S. He also led the integration of Gulf Power and launched award-winning initiatives aimed at education, workforce development, and community resilience.

A veteran in navigating the intersection of business and government, Silagy has held leadership roles with the Florida Chamber of Commerce, Florida Council of 100, and the State University System Board of Governors, where he helped oversee the state’s $4.5 billion university system budget. His experience ranges from coordinating statewide storm response efforts to championing critical infrastructure upgrades such as undergrounding power lines.

“I moved to Florida over 20 years ago and have come to care deeply about this state,” said Silagy. “Joining Related Ross is a chance to help Florida ‘set the table’ for a new generation—creating a city and region where the next chapter is defined not by inherited challenges, but by thoughtful, inclusive progress. What excites me most is being part of an organization that’s fully invested in the long-term future of our communities, not just the next project.”

As Vice Chairman, Silagy will work closely with elected officials, institutional partners, and business leaders to advance policies and initiatives that strengthen the region’s innovation economy. His responsibilities will include attracting new corporate tenants, securing support for major community-focused projects—such as those involving Vanderbilt University and the Cleveland Clinic—and representing Related Ross on civic panels and industry platforms throughout the state.

The move reflects Related Ross’ broader vision for shaping South Florida’s future through investment in education, healthcare, and workforce mobility. With ongoing developments like South Flagler House, The Laurel, and Shorecrest, as well as major commercial holdings and cultural hubs like CityPlace and Hilton West Palm Beach, the firm continues to lead the region’s transformation.

Silagy’s appointment reinforces that vision, positioning Related Ross to expand its role not only as a real estate leader, but as a long-term civic partner committed to building an economically vibrant, resilient, and inclusive South Florida.
