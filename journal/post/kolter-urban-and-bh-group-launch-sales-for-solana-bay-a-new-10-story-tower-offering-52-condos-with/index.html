Kolter Urban and BH Group launch sales for Solana Bay, a new 10-story tower offering 52 condos with panoramic views of Biscayne Bay in North Miami

By Jake Nicholas April 3, 2024

In a landmark collaboration, Kolter Urban and BH Group have proudly announced the unveiling of Solana Bay in Miami. This unique condominium project, situated at 2305 Northeast 123rd Street, promises a rare blend of luxury and privacy with its boutique design. It consists of 52 residences across 10 floors, each offering panoramic views of Biscayne Bay, stretching from Bal Harbour and Indian Creek to the bustling heart of Downtown Miami.

This project marks a significant partnership between Kolter Urban, known for its real estate development prowess based in Delray Beach, and BH Group, a distinguished real estate private equity entity rooted in Aventura. Solana Bay, nestled amidst Miami's sought-after coastal areas, aims to provide an exclusive waterfront living experience. Its location offers quick access to some of South Florida's top lifestyle hotspots, including Miami Beach, the Miami Design District, and Aventura.

Seth Goldman of Kolter Urban expressed enthusiasm about the venture, highlighting the project's strategic position to optimize the stunning views of the bay and city skyline. The development signifies a new phase in Miami's architectural evolution, enhancing the local charm with its innovative design and luxury offerings.

Designed by the internationally acclaimed Arquitectonica, the residences at Solana Bay are meticulously planned, ranging from two to four bedrooms and encompassing areas from 2,200 to over 4,000 square feet. The units mimic the spaciousness and privacy of single-family homes, featuring private entrances, large living areas, multipurpose spaces, master suites with scenic views, extensive terraces, and high-end finishes in kitchens and bathrooms. The building itself is a haven of wellness and luxury, housing a range of amenities focused on health, leisure, and community. These include a waterfront fitness center, a rooftop pool with panoramic views, and various spaces designed for relaxation and entertainment.

Isaac Toledano of BH Group emphasized the shared vision and commitment to excellence that defines the partnership with Kolter Urban, expressing anticipation for the project's success. Solana Bay stands as a testament to their dedication to creating premier condominiums in desirable Southeastern U.S. locations.

The project's sales and marketing efforts are spearheaded by Douglas Elliman Development Marketing, with private viewings scheduled to commence at the Presentation Gallery in North Miami starting mid-April 2024. Interested parties are encouraged to secure their private presentation by visiting SolanaBay.com, heralding a new era of luxury condominium living in Miami.
