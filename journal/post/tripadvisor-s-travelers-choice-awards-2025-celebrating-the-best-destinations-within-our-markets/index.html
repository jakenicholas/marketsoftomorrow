Tripadvisor’s Travelers’ Choice® Awards 2025: Celebrating the best destinations within our markets

Tripadvisor®, the world's largest travel guidance platform, has unveiled its first Travelers' Choice® Awards category for 2025: Best of...

By Jake Nicholas January 11, 2025

Tripadvisor®, the world's largest travel guidance platform, has unveiled its first Travelers' Choice® Awards category for 2025: Best of the Best Destinations. The awards highlight the top destinations globally and within the U.S., based on millions of reviews from travelers who’ve shared their experiences over the past year.

West Palm Beach’s Moment in the Spotlight

West Palm Beach, named the No. 1 Trending Destination in the U.S. for 2025 by TripAdvisor, is gaining momentum as a top spot for both leisure and business, earning nicknames like "Wall Street South" and "the center of everything." The area's affluence is rapidly growing, with West Palm Beach and Palm Beach ranking among the Top 5 fastest-growing areas for millionaires.

With major projects like the $1 billion Nora District and Flamingo Park’s Restaurant Row—set to feature the nation’s highest concentration of acclaimed restaurateurs —West Palm Beach continues to grow in influence.

Ultra-luxury residential and hospitality projects continue to be announced, including Alba Palm Beach, Mr C Residences, South Flagler House, The Berkeley, The Belgrove, Panther National, CityPlace, Eataly, and the new Convention Center Hotel.

Adding to the momentum, Vanderbilt University has announced plans to expand into downtown West Palm Beach with a graduate school campus. This expansion is attracting interest from other major players, including Silicon Valley-based companies exploring the area for its new headquarters. And with high-profile figures like Donald Trump and Elon Musk frequently in town, the city is solidifying its position as “the center of everything”.

NYC Takes the Crown as the Top U.S. Destination

New York City, a hub of culture, entertainment, and iconic landmarks, earned the top spot in the U.S. rankings. Known for its world-class dining and unmatched energy, NYC was also celebrated as one of the nation’s best food destinations. From Broadway's bright lights to the timeless charm of Central Park, NYC continues to capture the hearts of travelers year after year.

Nashville’s Musical Magic

Music City earned its place as No. 6 on the U.S. list, with its blend of vibrant live music, historic landmarks, and Southern hospitality. From the iconic Grand Ole Opry to the buzzing streets of Broadway, Nashville’s rich culture and energetic vibe continue to attract visitors from around the globe.

The Florida Keys: A Beloved Escape

Ranked No. 4 among U.S. destinations for 2025, the Florida Keys continue to enchant travelers with their unparalleled natural beauty and laid-back charm. Stretching over 100 miles, this island chain is famous for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and stunning sunsets. The Keys offer an array of activities, from world-class fishing and diving to exploring the historic streets of Key West.

Leading the way for travel is the newly opened Three Waters Resort & Marina, a Tribute Portfolio Resort, and the latest addition to the Islamorada Resort Collection. Spanning 15 acres of oceanfront property, the resort brings 214 island-inspired rooms, nine culinary venues, and expansive event spaces. It brings The Florida Keys a luxury experience with a focus on wellness and maritime activities, including boating and fishing lessons.

With its tropical allure and unique culture, the Florida Keys remain a perennial favorite for those seeking a relaxing escape, earning their place as one of the top destinations in the country.

Celebrating 25 Years of Travel Insights

Tripadvisor’s 2025 Travelers’ Choice® Awards also unveiled the best of the best destinations worldwide, as millions of reviews from global travelers help determine the top locations. London, UK, emerged as the No. 1 destination globally. The British capital has not only earned its spot for its rich cultural offerings but also for its diverse and vibrant culinary scene, where it ranks second in the world for food. Additionally, London’s continued prominence in Tripadvisor’s rankings is solidified as it tops the list for the last 25 years of traveler reviews, highlighting its long-standing appeal.

Following London, Bali claims second place on the list, continuing to captivate travelers with its stunning landscapes, spiritual sites, and unique cultural experiences. Dubai, U.A.E., holds onto the third spot, while Italy's Sicily and Paris, France, round out the top five. These destinations shine not only for their natural beauty but also for the experiences they offer, from historical landmarks to vibrant local cultures, making them favorites for travelers seeking unforgettable adventures.

Looking Ahead

With destinations like NYC, the Florida Keys, Nashville, and West Palm Beach making waves, 2025 promises exciting travel opportunities. These standout locations reflect the vibrancy and diversity of our featured markets, showcasing the best of what each region has to offer. Whether it’s the cultural allure of NYC, the tropical charm of the Keys, the soulful melodies of Nashville, or the coastal elegance of West Palm Beach, these destinations are set to inspire unforgettable journeys.
