The Palm Beaches

Inside look at the thirty-six seaside homes at Palm Beach Shores’ HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

Nestled on 2.5 acres along the Intracoastal Waterway in Palm Beach Shores, a new standard for luxury living is setting sail. HAVN...

By Jake Nicholas September 30, 2024

Nestled on 2.5 acres along the Intracoastal Waterway in Palm Beach Shores, a new standard for luxury living is setting sail. HAVN Residences & Yacht Club, developed by Johnston Group Capital Management, has unveiled its stunning model residence, designed by the acclaimed Interiors by Steven G.

Image Courtesy of HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

Imagine stepping into a light and airy haven, bathed in shades of white and accented by soft blues. This is the coastal aesthetic that permeates the meticulously designed space, featuring furniture that blends textures like leather, wicker, and boucle fabrics. Lighting fixtures adorned with shells, wooden beads, and jute complete the ambiance, a testament to Steven G.'s goal of enhancing the distinctive spaces crafted by Johnston Group.

Image Courtesy of HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

But HAVN Residences offers much more than breathtaking aesthetics. Residents can indulge in a resort-style lifestyle with a waterfront pool, hot tub, and fire pit overlooking the marina and Intracoastal. For the fitness enthusiasts, a modern gym beckons, while a serene courtyard garden provides a green escape.

Image Courtesy of HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

Stepping inside the residences themselves, a sense of spaciousness prevails. Private elevator entrances lead to homes featuring wood plank porcelain tile, hurricane impact windows, and fully-equipped laundry rooms. Modern kitchens boast custom shaker-style cabinets with top-of-the-line appliances by SubZero and Wolf, while luxurious bathrooms offer double vanity sinks, frameless showers, and, in many units, a standalone soaking tub. Sizes range from a comfortable 1,832 square feet to a sprawling 2,662 square feet, each featuring balconies with captivating views.

"Once you drive over the bridge, it truly feels like you're on vacation," says Carlyle Tiller, chief investment officer of Johnston Group. "We wanted that feeling to transcend the destination and into our residences."  

And for those who crave life on the water, HAVN seamlessly integrates yachting into the luxurious lifestyle. Residents enjoy priority access to the 52-slip, full-service marina managed by F3 Marina, complete with a stocked marina store, fuel and pump-out service, and a dedicated dock master and maintenance team. Recently to further elevate the experience, HAVN Yacht Club partnered with Admiral’s Club which features dedicated on-site professionals who provide personalized services such as scheduling vendor work, system monitoring, managing warranty follow-ups, and ensuring your vessel is always launch-ready with pre-launch preparation, including provisioning, fueling, and cleaning. With easy access to the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf Stream, boaters can embark on endless nautical adventures.

Image Courtesy of HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

Priced from $1.7 million, HAVN Residences promises a life of unparalleled luxury and convenience. Anticipated deliveries are set for Q4 2024. For more information, visit and register on their website at www.havnresidences.com or call them directly at 561-589-4422. Don't miss your chance to own a piece of paradise at HAVN Residences & Yacht Club today!
