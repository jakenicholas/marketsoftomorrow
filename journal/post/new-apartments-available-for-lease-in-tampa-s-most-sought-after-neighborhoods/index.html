New apartments available for lease in Tampa's most sought-after neighborhoods

By Jake Nicholas August 26, 2024

NOVEL Beach Park

NOVEL Beach Park, a newly opened waterfront oasis in Tampa, offers more than just luxurious living spaces. It's a lifestyle destination where residents can indulge in a variety of amenities designed to enhance their everyday experience.

Beyond its lavish interiors, NOVEL Beach Park boasts a serene Old Tampa Bay waterside pool and lounge, a 9th-floor Sunset Lounge with panoramic views, and a state-of-the-art fitness club. The unique Gear Lounge allows residents to borrow equipment for outdoor activities, and pet lovers will appreciate the pet-friendly amenities.

The homes themselves feature smart home technology, spacious balconies, and luxury plank flooring, while the kitchens and bathrooms are designed with both style and functionality in mind.

Conveniently located near a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options, NOVEL Beach Park provides residents with easy access to everything they need and more.

NOVEL Beach Park is not just an apartment complex; it's a vibrant community where luxury living meets coastal charm. With prices starting at $2,210/mo, NOVEL Beack Park offers the first month free.

La Unión

La Union Ybor, nearing completion as part of the first phase of Gas Worx, has commenced pre-leasing for its 316 apartments. Situated in a prime location between Gas Worx, Water Street, Encore, Ybor City, and Downtown Tampa, La Union offers an enticing blend of urban convenience and community-centric living.

With a range of floor plans, from studios to three-bedrooms, and competitive pricing starting at $1,800 per month, La Union caters to a diverse range of lifestyles. The community boasts an array of amenities, including electric car charging stations, a rooftop lounge, resort-style pool, and pet spa.

Each apartment features designer finishes, private balconies, and modern conveniences. The exclusive penthouse level offers elevated living with premium views. Move-ins are expected to begin in Summer 2024, welcoming residents to a vibrant and connected lifestyle in the heart of Tampa.

Asher Residences

Asher Residences, a new community in Water Street Tampa, redefines urban living with its focus on vibrant communal spaces and modern design. The collection of studio, one, and two-bedroom apartments are flooded with natural light through dramatic, oversized windows, fostering an airy and inviting atmosphere.

The seventh-floor amenity level offers a range of spaces designed to enhance residents' lifestyles, including a heated resort-style pool with spa, a state-of-the-art fitness center, social lounges, a co-working space, and a rec lawn with outdoor bar and BBQ.

With prices starting at $2,008 per month, Asher Residences provides a dynamic and engaging living experience in the heart of Tampa, where community and connection are prioritized.

101 N Meridian

101 N. Meridian, a newly opened residential community in Tampa's vibrant Channelside District, redefines urban living with a focus on connection and community. Its innovative design fosters interaction through thoughtfully curated amenity spaces, such as a 15th-floor sky lounge, outdoor pool deck, fitness center, and retreat terrace.

The smart home-enabled studios, one-, two-, and three-bedroom apartments and townhomes feature premium finishes, including Italian cabinetry, quartz countertops, and wood plank flooring, complemented by sweeping views of downtown and Water Street.

With studios starting at $1,950 per month, 101 N. Meridian presents an enticing option for those seeking a dynamic and engaging lifestyle in the heart of Tampa.

AER

AER redefines luxury living in Tampa's vibrant Arts & Entertainment District, offering a harmonious blend of modern design and classic elegance. The community fosters a balanced lifestyle with dynamic co-working spaces, a serene wellness lounge, and a range of studio to three-bedroom apartments.

Residents can enjoy a curated cityscape, blending artistry and nature, while indulging in upscale amenities such as a two-story lobby, rooftop fitness center, and an 11th-floor pool deck with panoramic views.

With prices starting at $2,995 per month, AER provides an exceptional living experience in the heart of Tampa's cultural scene, where every moment is a celebration of the extraordinary.
