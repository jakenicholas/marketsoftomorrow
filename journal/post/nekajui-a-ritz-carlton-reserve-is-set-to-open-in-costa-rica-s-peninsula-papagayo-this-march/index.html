Costa Rica

Nekajui, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve is set to open in Costa Rica’s Peninsula Papagayo this March

Opening March 1, 2025, Nekajui, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve, is ready to bring a new level of luxury to the region in Costa Rica’s Peninsula...

By Kaitlyn Nicholas February 6, 2025

Opening March 1, 2025, Nekajui, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve, is ready to bring a new level of luxury to the region in Costa Rica’s Peninsula Papagayo, a 1,400-acre private gated community known for its luxury resorts, world-class marina, beach clubs, and an Arnold Palmer-designed golf course. This high-end retreat will feature 107 accommodations, including suites, private residences, and three luxury treetop tents, each harmonizing with the landscape while offering modern amenities like private plunge pools and panoramic terraces with views of Pochote Bay.

The resort’s architectural vision is deeply influenced by Costa Rica’s heritage, divided into three unique zones. The classic zone presents a modernized take on traditional hacienda aesthetics, the transitional zone incorporates regional design elements, and the organic zone showcases elevated structures that blend into the natural environment. The heart of the resort, La Casona, is a sophisticated nod to the region’s ranching traditions.

Guests will have access to top-tier amenities, including the 27,000-square-foot Nimbu Spa, home to the largest hydrotherapy pool in Latin America. Dining options include Puna, the resort’s signature restaurant, a hidden speakeasy, an artisan café and market, and Ambar, a treetop bar with sweeping ocean views. Niri Beach Club, a secluded coastal escape, is reachable via a private funicular and features a beachfront dining experience centered around a wood-fired oven.

For those seeking adventure, Nekajui provides personalized itineraries led by local specialists. Guests can explore the region’s natural wonders through Jean-Michel Cousteau’s Ambassadors of the Environment program, which offers guided experiences across protected ecosystems. The bay’s bioluminescent waters and calm conditions also make it a prime location for water activities.

Developed by Gencom in collaboration with Peninsula Papagayo, Nekajui joins the prestigious Ritz-Carlton Reserve collection, which includes properties in Bali, Thailand, Japan, Mexico, and Puerto Rico. In addition to the resort, 36 private residences and the exclusive 10-bedroom Villa Guayacan present an opportunity for those seeking high-end coastal living. These fully furnished homes, ranging from two to five bedrooms, feature expansive canopied terraces, private plunge pools, and architecture that reflects the cultural heritage of Costa Rica. Priced between $3 million and $13 million, these residences cater to those looking for a rare and prestigious retreat. Designed with collectors of exceptional properties in mind, each home seamlessly integrates luxury with the natural surroundings while embracing traditional Tico design influences.
