Inside Palm Beach Shores' newest waterfront community HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

With just months to go until completion, HAVN Residences & Yacht Club is entering its final stage, bringing a new era of luxury...

By Jake Nicholas February 13, 2025

With just months to go until completion, HAVN Residences & Yacht Club is entering its final stage, bringing a new era of luxury waterfront living to Palm Beach Shores. Johnston Group Capital Management, Inc. is thrilled to announce that this exclusive community remains on track for its grand debut in April 2025, offering an exceptional blend of sophisticated design, world-class amenities, and an unbeatable location.

Interior renovations are advancing swiftly, with 25 Temporary Certificates of Occupancy (TCOs) issued for waterfront residences and a Certificate of Occupancy (CO) granted for the model residence. The remaining residences are on track for completion by March 2025.

Enhancements to common areas are also progressing as planned:

  • New Roof Installation: Completed across all five buildings in December 2024.

  • Pool Renovations: Initiated in January, with an expected completion by March 2025.

  • Fitness Center Remodel: A state-of-the-art facility set to open in April 2025.

  • Parking Area Updates: Resealing and striping are scheduled for April 2025.

Palm Beach Shores, affectionately known as the "Best Little Town in Florida," offers its residents a unique blend of exclusivity and tranquility. One of the town's prized amenities is its residents-only beach, ensuring a serene coastal experience away from the typical tourist paths. This private stretch of sand allows locals to enjoy the pristine Atlantic shoreline in a peaceful setting.

A notable feature is the scenic walking path that runs east-west towards the Palm Beach Inlet. This trail begins near a large fountain at Bamboo Road and Park Avenue and concludes at a historic jetty, which marks the easternmost point of Florida. Along the way, walkers and cyclists are treated to views of massive banyan trees, charming vintage buildings, and glimpses of the Lake Worth Lagoon and the Atlantic Ocean. For dining and entertainment, residents frequent the Sailfish Marina Resort, a local gem set on the water. This resort offers a delightful blend of Old Florida charm and modern amenities.

HAVN Residences & Yacht Club offers residents unparalleled access to a variety of water activities and a day dock for pick up and drop off right at their doorstep. Just a short distance away lies Peanut Island, a renowned destination for snorkeling and scuba diving enthusiasts. The island's clear, shallow waters are teeming with vibrant marine life, including tropical fish, rays, and occasionally manatees, making it ideal for both novice and experienced snorkelers. Visitors can explore the island's snorkeling lagoon, swim, or simply relax on its pristine beaches.

NORA District: An upcoming $1 billion mixed-use destination in West Palm Beach, NORA is set to redefine urban living with a blend of adaptive reuse and new construction. The district will feature a curated mix of dining, retail, fitness, and entertainment venues, all designed within a pedestrian-friendly environment.

The District at Northwood: Known for its eclectic mix of galleries, boutiques, and eateries, the Northwood District offers a vibrant arts scene and hosts regular community events, making it a cultural hub in West Palm Beach, with a massive new mixed-use district arriving in early 2026.

Downtown West Palm Beach: A bustling and growing urban center with popular areas like Clematis Street and CityPlace. Downtown West Palm Beach offers a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options, as well as a lively waterfront area that hosts concerts and festivals throughout the year. Vanderbilt University also just announced its $520 million downtown campus arriving in the coming years.

Palm Beach International Airport: HAVN Residences & Yacht Club offers residents the advantage of being only a 15-minute drive to one of the country’s best-rated small airports, Palm Beach International Airport (PBI). PBI, renowned for its passenger-friendly amenities, including a variety of dining and shopping options, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, and the Escape Lounges – The Centurion® Studio Partner - The airport also sees some of the quickest security wait times in Florida.

Brightline Station: This modern transportation hub, just over the bridge in Downtown West Palm Beach provides high-speed rail services connecting West Palm Beach to key Florida destinations, including Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, and Orlando.

John D. MacArthur Beach State Park: As the only state park in Palm Beach County, John D. MacArthur Beach State Park encompasses 1.6 miles of pristine beach on a barrier island just a few miles from HAVN Residences & Yacht Club. The park is home to a unique mixture of coastal and tropical hammock and mangrove forest, providing a haven for several rare native tropical and coastal plant species. Visitors can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, hiking, and birdwatching, as well as explore the park's nature center and participate in various educational programs.

"With construction milestones progressing ahead of schedule, this is an exciting phase for HAVN as we approach completion," said Ryan Johnston, CEO of Johnston Group. "Bringing these residences to market so quickly presents a rare opportunity for buyers—perfectly timed as South Florida enters peak season. Our commitment to redefining luxury in Palm Beach Shores is evident in every detail of HAVN, from design to lifestyle amenities."

HAVN Residences seamlessly blends modern design with coastal elegance, offering 36 two- and three-bedroom residences featuring semi-private elevator foyers, hurricane-impact windows, and premium finishes. Kitchens are equipped with custom shaker-style cabinetry, Quartz countertops, Sub-Zero® and Wolf® appliances, and wine storage. Primary bathrooms boast frameless showers, double vanities with illuminated mirrors, and soaking tubs in select layouts.

HAVN will enjoy resort-style amenities including a waterside pool, spa, marina, fire pit, and concierge services, along with complimentary bicycles for easy exploration, kayaks, and paddle boards for navigating the Intracoastal and nearby waterways, and a chauffeured golf cart for convenient access to further points. HAVN residents will also enjoy Palm Beach Shores resident-only beach access offering complimentary beach chairs and umbrellas.

The adjacent full-service marina, managed by F3 Marina, offers 52 slips accommodating vessels up to 150 feet, with priority leasing available for residents.

Pricing for HAVN Residences starts at $1.25 million, with notable sales activity, including the sale of a penthouse listed at $3.62 million. The first model residence, designed by award-winning Interiors by Steven G., is open for tours.

Located at 184 Lake Drive, Palm Beach Shores, HAVN Residences & Yacht Club offers direct access to the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean, just minutes to the inlet and out to the open waters of the Atlantic Ocean. Compass Development Marketing Group oversees sales for the project.
