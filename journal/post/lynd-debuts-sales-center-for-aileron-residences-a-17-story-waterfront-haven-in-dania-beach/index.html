LYND debuts Sales Center for Aileron Residences, a 17-story waterfront haven in Dania Beach

The LYND Company (LYND) is excited to introduce Aileron Residences, a 17-story tower set to transform Dania Beach, Florida. The local...

By Jake Nicholas November 21, 2024

The LYND Company (LYND) is excited to introduce Aileron Residences, a 17-story tower set to transform Dania Beach, Florida. The local real estate market has experienced remarkable growth, resulting in the creation of various new employment opportunities. Dania Beach is a major player in South Florida’s academic, marine, and tourism industries. Inspired by aviation and travel, this dynamic project perfectly blends urban vibrancy with tranquil coastal living.

Rendering Credit: Aileron Residence, Nichols Architect

Conveniently positioned near Dania Beach Pier, downtown Fort Lauderdale, and the Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, the development offers 380 studio-to-three-bedroom units and townhomes ranging from 409 to 2,847 square feet. Pricing ranges from $350,000 to just under $2,000,000, making it an attainable luxury option for most, and will launch in the first quarter of 2024. Compass Development Marketing Group is the exclusive sales and marketing team for the project. 

"LYND and the entire development team are thrilled to launch Aileron Residences and contribute to the evolution and development of Dania Beach," said A. David Lynd, President and Chief Executive Officer of LYND. “From spacious condos to world-class amenities, Aileron offers the very best value proposition for your money. We are confident that our project will be successful and benefit the city for years to come.” 

Rendering Credit: Aileron Residence, Nichols Architect & Hillary Littlejohn Studio Design

Situated at 90 North Bryan Road in Dania Beach, the architecture for the development is by world-renowned Nichols Architects. Taking inspiration from an aircraft's aileron, the building is a testament to innovation, providing affordable luxury. Inside the building, the residences feature the perfect solution for those looking for functional elegance in studio condominiums, scaling up to spacious 3-bedroom floor plans and townhomes, all showcasing top-notch finishes and amenities. 

Rendering Credit: Aileron Residence, Hillary Littlejohn Studio Design

"Aileron is an incredible balance between modernity and timelessness and differentiates itself as a sanctuary of contemporary comfort with a unique flair," said Marisela Cotilla, Senior Managing Director of Compass Development Marketing Group. "We have seen a major increase in buyers looking for a highly amenitized offering just outside a busy downtown, with the accessibility and amenities of a walkable city, all at an attainable price point. We are excited for future residents to experience all Dania Beach has to offer." 

The interiors from Hillary Littlejohn Studio Design are crafted with light and materiality in mind. Units feature large windows, hardwood floors, and private balconies. A calming color palette of warm, grey, and cool white stone and tile accompanies spacious closets and exquisite shower fittings in the primary bedrooms. The kitchens feature impeccable design, integrated lighting, and waterfall counters, along with custom countertops and sleek backsplashes. Bathrooms feature LED-illuminated mirrors, elegant tile showers, and a relaxing bathtub surrounded by luxury fixtures.

Rendering Credit: Aileron Residence, Hillary Littlejohn Studio Design

"In the vibrant landscape of Dania, this project will stand as a testament to unparalleled value—where each dollar invested promises a return beyond comparison,” said Lynd. “With Aileron, we’re redefining luxury living by granting every resident access to an amenity set usually reserved for 1,000-square-foot condos, making the Florida luxury lifestyle an everyday reality." 

Aileron will provide its residents with access to state-of-the-art amenities. Boat rentals, a boardwalk, and boat slips are all available along with the benefits of being on the water. LYND, a company that values community, has carefully designed amenity spaces that cater to the interests of future residents. Working closely with HLS Design, LYND created a range of elevated amenities, including fitness and yoga wellness spaces with infrared heat.

Rendering Credit: Aileron Residence, Hillary Littlejohn Studio Design

Additionally, LYND has taken into account the needs of work-from-home residents by providing a dynamic co-working space with individual workstations and "huddle rooms" for small group meetings. HLS Design contributed to the creation of the community gourmet kitchen, perfect for hosting parties, cooking classes, and community events. Communal spaces like “The Lounge” and “The Pool Cabana” are available for residents to relax, watch sports games, or enjoy a bottle of wine with friends. With its stunning sunrise and sunset pools, breathtaking views, and lush landscaping, the outdoor pool deck is truly one-of-a-kind, thanks to South Florida's beautiful weather. 

Rendering Credit: Dania Pointe

In addition to providing a dynamic living space, the development also offers 33,000 square feet of retail space on the ground level along the “Marina Boardwalk.” The developers and commercial real estate team are working to create a unique atmosphere that will enhance the community and contribute to the larger Dania Pointe master plan. The city is already home to 30,000 residents, as well as the American Maritime Officers (AMO) Simulation, Training, Assessment, and Research Center and the Derecktor Shipyards, which is one of South Florida's largest yacht repair centers. Additionally, the city boasts a 120-slip marina located between the Intracoastal and the Atlantic Ocean. 

For more information about Aileron Residences, visit https://aileronresidences.com
