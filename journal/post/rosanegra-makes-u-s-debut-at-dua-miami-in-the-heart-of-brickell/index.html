RosaNegra makes U.S. debut at Dua Miami in the heart of Brickell

Grupo RosaNegra , Mexico’s leading hospitality group known for its signature fusion of high-energy ambiance and elevated Latin American...

By Jake Nicholas December 16, 2024

Grupo RosaNegra, Mexico’s leading hospitality group known for its signature fusion of high-energy ambiance and elevated Latin American cuisine, just opened RosaNegra in Miami on December 4, 2024.

Located within the new Dua Miami, Autograph Collection Hotel (formerly SLS Brickell), this opening marks the group’s highly anticipated expansion into the U.S., joining the brand’s successful roster of flagship locations in Mexico, including Polanco, Tulum, Cancun, and Los Cabos. A favorite of high-profile names like Karol G, Canelo Álvarez, Eva Longoria, and David Beckham, RosaNegra offers an unparalleled dining experience in the heart of Miami's vibrant Brickell district.

"Miami was the natural choice for our first U.S. outpost," said Eduardo Beaven, CEO and Founder of Grupo RosaNegra. "The city’s rich culture and culinary diversity perfectly align with our vision to deliver bold Latin flavors, elevated dining, and unforgettable, high-energy experiences. We are thrilled to be able to bring the essence of RosaNegra to such a dynamic and globally connected community.”

RosaNegra Miami offers an elevated Latin American menu with influences from the rich gastronomic traditions of Mexico, Peru, Argentina, Colombia, and Brazil. In addition to beloved RosaNegra classics, the Miami location offers a unique culinary program that reflects the city’s energy and vibrant food scene. The menu leads with enticing appetizers such as Burrata & Mole, Tuna Tostadas, and a range of raw bar items Shrimp Carpaccio, Salmon Tiradito and Green Ceviche with aguachile. Standout signature dishes include dramatic Lucifer Tomahawk on Fire, Super Colossal Octopus, and Orzo Pasta with Ribs. The menu also features a premium beef selection, including Kobe beef, Japanese wagyu, and an array of USDA prime-quality Argentinian cuts.

 RosaNegra’s beverage program pays homage to Latin America’s diverse spirit culture. Signature cocktails include the Paradise in the Jungle (Brazilian cachaça, coconut liqueur, lemon juice, and avocado); La Lola (Charanda-aged rum from Michoacán, passion fruit, lemon juice, and a clarified strawberry foam); and Luna (Bacanora, an agave-based spirit from Sonora, orange liqueur, roasted pineapple, turmeric, green tomato, and basil). Complementing the cocktails, the menu also features an extensive selection of Mexican wines, premium tequilas, and mezcals. Guests can look forward to the high-energy ambiance that the RosaNegra brand is famous for, complete with live instrument performances and a resident DJ, creating a seamless transition from a refined dining experience to an exhilarating late-night atmosphere.

Designed by Clavel Arquitectos and Grupo RosaNegra’s CEO and Founder, Eduardo Beaven, RosaNegra Miami spans an impressive 13,600 square feet—including 4,600 square feet of outdoor dining space. The venue exudes a modern, elegant ambiance, marrying natural elements with contemporary design. Warm, earthy tones, a wooden ceiling, and expansive windows fill the dining area with natural light, creating an inviting yet sophisticated environment. The bar area is accentuated by neutral lighting and framed by captivating artwork that distinguishes all of RosaNegra’s locations. An expansive outdoor patio, adorned with woven basket chandeliers, lush greenery, and plush seating, offers an idyllic setting for al fresco dining. Beyond the main dining area, guests will also discover an exclusive speakeasy, offering a secluded, intimate retreat for savoring expertly crafted cocktails. 

 RosaNegra is open daily for dinner service starting at 5 pm, with brunch and lunch to launch in the coming weeks. Nightly programming will roll out in the coming months. RosaNegra is located at 1346 S Miami Ave, Miami, FL 33130. For more information and upcoming announcements, visit: rosanegrarestaurant.us. Follow Rosa Negra on social: @rosanegramiami

About Grupo RosaNegra

Founded in 2017 by visionary Eduardo Beaven, Grupo RosaNegra is a renowned hospitality brand dedicated to celebrating Latin American culture through its exceptional culinary offerings and immersive dining experiences.  In just a few years, it has expanded its brands to some of Mexico's and Spain’s top destinations. The group is known for creating exceptionally creative and captivating concepts, including the highly successful RosaNegra, Taboo Restaurants & Beach Clubs, Parole, Chambao, and Mamazzita, among many others—each with its own unique style and personality.
