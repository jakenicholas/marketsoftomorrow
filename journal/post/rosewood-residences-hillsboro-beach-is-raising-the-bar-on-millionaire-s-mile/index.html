Rosewood Residences Hillsboro Beach is raising the bar on "Millionaire's Mile"

Hillsboro Beach, affectionately known as "Millionaire's Mile," has long attracted affluent homeowners seeking a serene, waterfront...

By Jake Nicholas February 20, 2025

Hillsboro Beach, affectionately known as "Millionaire's Mile," has long attracted affluent homeowners seeking a serene, waterfront lifestyle with luxurious residential enclaves and pristine white-sand beaches. Offering only 92 residences, Rosewood Residences Hillsboro Beach continues this tradition of exclusivity and desirability, with developers Related Group and Dezer Development driving the five-star vision. 

Rosewood Residences Hillsboro Beach is more than just a condominium; it embodies the global legacy of the Rosewood brand. The architecture, crafted by the internationally acclaimed firm Arquitectonica, advances the bold modernism that has defined South Florida over the last 40 years, integrating organic materials and overall emphasis on biophilic design. Inspired by Rosewood’s A Sense of Place™ philosophy, residents of Rosewood Residences Hillsboro Beach experience a seamless blend of sophisticated aesthetics and a deep attention to detail. The design draws from the elegance of a regal yacht, blending clean lines and curving terraces with ample natural light and sprawling exterior areas. 

Inside, the esteemed Dutch design firm Studio Piet Boon continues that approach, with meticulously crafted interiors that reflect the Rosewood brand’s signature style. The soft color palette and rich mix of textures, including natural wood, limestone accents, and ample greenery, create luxurious environments that are visually stunning, deeply comfortable, and uniquely connected to nature.

Rosewood's dedication to quality and enduring style is evident throughout its projects, with this residential tower being a true expression of understated luxury.

“Today’s buyers are not just searching for a residence; they want a lifestyle that blends the personal comfort of a private home with the luxury of a high-end resort,” said Wendy Marks, Sales Director for Related Group. “Rosewood Residences is designed to offer a unique blend of privacy and elegance, enhanced by a suite of world-class amenities. This project exemplifies our commitment to creating unparalleled beachfront living that combines sophistication with a true sense of retreat.”

The property features 70 beachfront residences and 22 coastal residences, all thoughtfully designed to enhance their breathtaking surroundings. With sizes ranging from 2,800 to 6,400 square feet, the residences are adaptable, allowing for the combination of units to create expansive coastal retreats.

Wellness and relaxation are integral to the design, with a state-of-the-art wellness center featuring a private spa and salon, yoga rooms, and fitness facilities. The property also offers an exclusive oceanfront pool, outdoor spa areas with sunbeds and cabanas, and direct access to a fully serviced private beach.

“Rosewood Residences in Hillsboro Beach sets a new standard for luxury design,” said Nick Pérez, President of Related Group’s Condominium Division. “We’ve combined the distinct elegance of the Rosewood brand with the natural beauty of the coastal setting to create a living experience that’s both visually stunning and deeply serene. This development reflects our dedication to crafting environments where high-end design and peaceful coastal living seamlessly come together.”

Residents and their guests can also enjoy elevated dining experiences, curated by a renowned chef, including private oceanfront dining, a sophisticated cocktail lounge, and a café lounge with a daily breakfast bar.

Rosewood Residences Hillsboro Beach is thoughtfully designed for all lifestyles, offering spaces for all ages, such as a game room with high-tech virtual reality simulators, meeting rooms, and a sunrise lounge ideal for entertaining. Additional amenities include bocce, paddle tennis, and pickleball courts, as well as an on-site dog park. The property will also feature a curated collection of museum-quality art, hand-selected by Related Group's art curatorial team.

Prices start at $5.8 million. For more information or to visit the sales gallery, please call (754) 260-3971 or visit ResidencesHillsboroBeach.com
