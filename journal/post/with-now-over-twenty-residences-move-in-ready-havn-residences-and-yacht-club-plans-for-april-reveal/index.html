HAVN Residences & Yacht Club, a luxury boutique waterfront oasis is officially move-in ready

Johnston Group Capital Management, Inc., an affiliate of Palm Beach Gardens-based Johnston Group Development & Construction, has reached...

By Jake Nicholas January 14, 2025

Johnston Group Capital Management, Inc., an affiliate of Palm Beach Gardens-based Johnston Group Development & Construction, has reached significant milestones in the construction of HAVN Residences & Yacht Club, a waterfront luxury community in Palm Beach Shores. With construction coming to a close, the project remains on schedule for completion in April, delivering a new standard of yacht club living in South Florida.

Interior renovations have progressed swiftly, with 26 residences completed and granted temporary certificates of occupancy, including 16 finalized in late 2024. The remaining 10 residences are on track to be completed and delivered by Q2 of 2025.

Enhancements to the community’s common areas are also nearing completion:

  • Roof Replacement: Completed across all five buildings in December 2024.

  • Pool Renovations: Underway since early January, scheduled for completion in March 2025.

  • Fitness Center: A state-of-the-art remodel will debut alongside the community’s grand opening in April.

  • Parking Area Updates: Final resealing and striping will take place in April.

“With the finishing touches coming together, HAVN is truly beginning to take shape,” said Ryan Johnston, CEO of Johnston Group. “As South Florida enters its peak season, this project reflects our commitment to creating a lifestyle destination unlike any other in Palm Beach Shores. From the impeccable craftsmanship of each residence to the unparalleled amenities, HAVN represents a harmonious blend of luxury and coastal charm.”

A New Standard for Coastal Living Set across 2.5 acres, HAVN Residences blends modern design with coastal elegance, offering 36 two- and three-bedroom residences with private elevator entrances, expansive terraces, hurricane-impact windows, and premium finishes.

Kitchens are outfitted with custom shaker-style cabinetry, Quartz countertops, Sub-Zero® and Wolf® appliances, and wine storage, while primary bathrooms feature frameless showers, double vanities with illuminated mirrors, and soaking tubs in select layouts.

Complementing the residences are resort-style amenities, including a waterside pool, spa, fire pit, and marina views. Residents will also enjoy a fitness center, outdoor kitchen with gas grills, beach cabanas, concierge services, and complimentary access to kayaks, paddleboards, and bikes. The full-service marina, managed by F3 Marina, offers 52 slips for vessels up to 150 feet, along with priority leasing for residents.

Sales & Availability Pricing for HAVN Residences starts at $1.25 million, with notable sales activity, including a penthouse recently selling for $3.62 million. The first model residence, designed by award-winning Interiors by Steven G., is now open for tours.

Nestled at 184 Lake Drive in Palm Beach Shores, HAVN Residences & Yacht Club offers direct access to the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean, just half a mile from the inlet. Compass Development Marketing Group oversees sales for the project. Interested buyers can contact the sales team at 561-589-4422 or sales@HAVNResidences.com

For more information, visit www.HAVNResidences.com
