Eudemonia, a 3-day health and wellness summit is coming to West Palm Beach on November 1st

Discover the transformative power of Eudēmonia , a 3-day summit dedicated to empowering individuals to cultivate their well-being in all...

By Jake Nicholas October 2, 2024

Discover the transformative power of Eudēmonia, a 3-day summit dedicated to empowering individuals to cultivate their well-being in all aspects of life. Held November 1-3, 2024 in West Palm Beach, Florida, Eudēmonia, organized by the creators of Wanderlust, offers a comprehensive exploration of health and wellness, featuring expert talks, experiential activities, and networking opportunities.

Eudēmonia brings together a stellar lineup of renowned experts in the fields of health, fitness, and biology. Gain insights from the following thought leaders:

  • Andrew Huberman, PhD: Neuroscientist and Podcaster, known for his work at Stanford School of Medicine and the “Huberman Lab” podcast.

  • Adriene Mishler: International Yoga Teacher and co-founder of “Yoga with Adriene” and the @fwfglife app.

  • Mark Hyman, MD: Functional Medicine Pioneer, co-founder of  @functionHealth, and a leader in the field of functional medicine.

  • Gabrielle Lyon, DO: Muscle Health Expert, founder of Muscle-Centric Medicine® and advocate for optimal muscle health.

  • Siddartha Mukherjee, MD: Cell Researcher & AI Health Expert, known for his contributions to oncology and AI applications in health.

  • Jillian Michaels: Fitness Coach & Podcaster, renowned fitness expert and creator of @thefitnessapp.

  • Sara Gottfried, MD: Hormonal Health Expert, clinical professor specializing in integrative medicine and hormonal health.

  • Max Lugavere: Health Science Podcaster and author of the “Genius” trilogy, focusing on health optimization and lifestyle.

Immerse Yourself in Well-Being

Beyond expert talks, Eudēmonia offers a variety of immersive experiences designed to enhance your well-being. Engage in:

  • Fitness activities: From yoga andpPilates to strength training and high-intensity interval training.

  • Mindfulness practices: Meditation, breathwork, and guided visualizations.

  • Restorative treatments: Massage, acupuncture, and other therapies designed to promote relaxation and healing.

  • Spa experiences: Indulge in luxurious treatments and pampering.
