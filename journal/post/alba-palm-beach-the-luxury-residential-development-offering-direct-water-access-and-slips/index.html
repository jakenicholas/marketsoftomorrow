Alba Palm Beach, the luxury residential development offering direct water access and slips

By Jake Nicholas October 30, 2024

Alba Palm Beach has recently unveiled stunning new renderings of its highly anticipated lower penthouse and residences C and D, setting the stage for an extraordinary living experience. These exceptional homes feature expansive outdoor spaces that offer breathtaking, unobstructed views of the Intracoastal Waterway, enhancing the beauty and serenity of waterfront living. Intimately sized yet graciously appointed, these residences are centrally located directly on the Intracoastal, making them a dramatic step forward in luxurious coastal living.

Alba Palm Beach is designed by the acclaimed architectural firm Spina O'Rourke + Partners, who have thoughtfully crafted a collection of only 55 boutique residences. Each unit offers refined two- to four-bedroom floor plans, allowing residents to choose a layout that perfectly suits their lifestyle. The exclusive two- and three-story townhomes provide additional living options, combining elegance and functionality. With serene waterfront vistas and contemporary design elements, these residences promise to elevate your everyday life.

With over 50% of the development already pre-sold, construction is progressing swiftly and is on track for completion in Spring 2026. Units start at just under $3 million, reflecting the luxury and exclusivity that this development embodies.

One of the standout features of Alba Palm Beach is its impressive suite of over 25,000 square feet of residential on-site amenities. Residents will enjoy access to a variety of luxurious services and facilities, including a state-of-the-art fitness center, a serene spa, and beautifully landscaped gardens.

Additionally, owners will benefit from a complimentary membership to the coveted Palm Beach Yacht Club, providing an unparalleled opportunity for those who appreciate life on the water. This exclusive membership allows residents to enjoy the ultimate beachside escape, with direct access to boating and yachting activities.

Alba Palm Beach is situated in one of South Florida’s most prestigious neighborhoods, offering residents not just a home, but a lifestyle characterized by elegance and sophistication. The location provides easy access to vibrant downtown West Palm Beach, where fine dining, upscale shopping, and cultural attractions abound. Living at Alba means being just moments away from everything this dynamic city has to offer, while still enjoying the tranquility and beauty of waterfront living.

Don’t miss your chance to explore these one-of-a-kind residences in the heart of West Palm Beach. With limited availability and growing interest, now is the perfect time to schedule your private preview. Experience firsthand the luxurious lifestyle that awaits you at Alba Palm Beach—where every detail has been thoughtfully curated to create a truly exceptional living environment.
