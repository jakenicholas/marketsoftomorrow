PLANTA is launching their new plant-based burgers at their West Palm Beach and South Beach locations today, featuring jackfruit, and fried mushroom bacon

Today marks an exciting milestone for PLANTA as they unveil their groundbreaking Plant-Based Burger at both the West Palm Beach and South...

By Jake Nicholas February 22, 2024

Today marks an exciting milestone for PLANTA as they unveil their groundbreaking Plant-Based Burger at both the West Palm Beach and South Beach locations. Far from the conventional veggie burger, their culinary team has meticulously developed recipes that stand toe-to-toe with traditional meat burgers, including an innovative version featuring jackfruit, aiming to delight even the most refined tastes.

Introducing their new Burger Lineup:

Classic Burger: A perfect blend of patty, cheddar, mushroom bacon, and fresh produce, topped with our signature sauce.

Kentucky Burger: A bold mix of chick'n fried mushrooms, buffalo sauce, and ranch, accented with pickles and pickled peppers.

California Burger: A fusion of patty, cheddar, and smashed avocado, seasoned with hot peppers and our secret sauce.

Fire House Burger: A daring combination of patty, BBQ jackfruit, and pickled jalapeños, complemented by cheddar, caramelized onions, and chipotle aioli.

PLANTA isn't just a restaurant; it's a revolution in dining, championing inventive and delectable 100% plant-based cuisine since its inception in 2016 by visionaries Steven Salm and David Lee. Their mission? To democratize plant-based dining by making it irresistibly tasty and widely available.

Their menu is a testament to the endless possibilities of plant-based cooking, from starters and main courses to desserts. PLANTA prioritizes top-tier ingredients to craft dishes that are both mouth-watering and fulfilling. Delicacies like the Unagi Eggplant Nigiri and the Crispy Mushroom Hand Roll showcase their passion for Asian flavors, while the BAO Slider and Watermelon Tuna Roll bring a surprising delight to already loved dishes with a plant-based spin. And for dessert enthusiasts, their cheesecake rivals the finest out there

PLANTA is also deeply invested in sustainability and animal welfare, sourcing from eco-conscious farms and adopting green practices across our operations.

Be Part of the Change:

Experience our innovative burgers for pick-up and delivery at PLANTA West Palm Beach, South Beach, and Coconut Grove via Uber Eats, Toast, or the PLANTA Passport app.

West Palm Beach: The Square, 700 S Rosemary Ave Suite 142, West Palm Beach, FL 33401

South Beach: 850 Commerce St, Miami Beach, FL 33139

PLANTA: https://www.plantarestaurants.com/
