Inside the $2 billion St. Regis Resort and Residences, Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale megaproject

By Jake Nicholas March 6, 2025

Fort Lauderdale, celebrated as the 'yachting capital of the world’, is famed for its scenic waterways and immaculate beaches. Today, the city is undergoing a transformation, pairing its maritime heritage with an elevated sense of luxury. 

The St. Regis Resort and Residences, Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale — developed by Related Group, Tate Capital and Rok Acquisitions in collaboration with Marriott International — is the newest addition to the city’s luxury residential landscape. Spanning over 40 acres of land and water, this $2 billion development will elevate the premier Bahia Mar megayacht marina, home to the world’s largest in-water boat show, into a world-class lifestyle destination, complete with branded residential towers, a flagship St. Regis hotel, a private beach club and a selection of signature fine dining and upscale retail. 

This extraordinary development will unfold in two phases, transforming the site into a tropical sanctuary. The first phase will feature 160 condominium homes across two 23-story residential towers, alongside exclusive resort residences atop the 197-key hotel. Crafted by an elite design team, the visionary architecture is led by the renowned Arquitectonica, complemented by refined interiors from London-based Tara Bernerd & Partners.

The master plan includes 88,000 square feet of luxurious waterfront commercial space featuring restaurants and boat docking, a public park along the Intracoastal Waterway and a 25-foot-wide pedestrian promenade encircling the property’s west, north and south sides. An exclusive marina features slips for megayachts up to 350 feet.

“This visionary development is not only befitting of this one-of-a-kind site but also a critical addition to Fort Lauderdale’s broader evolution,” said Jorge M. Pérez, Chairman and CEO of Related Group. “With the introduction of The St. Regis Resort and Residences, Bahia Mar Fort Lauderdale, our team is cementing the city as one of the world’s luxury epicenters.”

The condominiums will include three- and four-bedroom options ranging from 2,600 to 3,550 square feet, plus penthouse residences. Designed for effortless living and entertaining, these light-filled residences showcase modern, open layouts and wraparound glass terraces that provide sweeping views of the Atlantic Ocean, Intracoastal Waterway and Fort Lauderdale skyline. The residences within the hotel will offer one to three bedrooms, in addition to penthouse options.

Daily life is enriched by 35,000 square feet of amenities in each building that include a spa with steam rooms, saunas, hot and cold plunges, treatment rooms and a wellness terrace; a salon; an indoor-outdoor fitness center; a media room; a private dining room and a billiards room. Lush terraces with summer kitchens; a private outdoor pool with a whirlpool; a pickleball court; a multisport simulator and a beach club enhance the experience.

Owners of the resort residences will also have access to the resort amenities, including two swimming pools, a fitness center, a spa with outdoor amenities and a signature waterfront restaurant. Pricing begins at $3 million for condominium and resort residences. For more information, visit www.srrbahiamar.com or call (954) 323-5072
