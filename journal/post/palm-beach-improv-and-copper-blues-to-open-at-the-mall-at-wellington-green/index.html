Palm Beach Improv and Copper Blues to open at The Mall at Wellington Green

Two renowned venues, Palm Beach Improv and Copper Blues Rock Pub & Kitchen, just announced that they are set to open at The Mall at...

By Jake Nicholas June 6, 2025

Two renowned venues, Palm Beach Improv and Copper Blues Rock Pub & Kitchen, just announced that they are set to open at The Mall at Wellington Green later this year. Occupying a combined 13,700-square-foot space on the mall’s upper level near CMX Cinemas, this duo plans to bring its dynamic blend of comedy, live music, and dining to Wellington.

Palm Beach Improv and Copper Blues have been a cornerstone of West Palm Beach's comedy scene for over two decades. Originally located in CityPlace in downtown West Palm Beach, the concepts were both demolished to make way for the next chapter of Related Ross' vision — the 10 & 15 CityPlace towers. These towers are set to bring a new era of office towers that blend into the rest of the district, with new and upcoming brands like Eataly, Alo, Equinox Fitness Club, Moxie's, and more.

After the demolition, Palm Beach Improv moved next door, temporarily, to the Kravis Center.

“This is a huge moment for Wellington,” said Asad Sadiq, General Manager of The Mall at Wellington Green. “We’re bringing nationally recognized entertainment and a fresh energy to the mall. Palm Beach Improv and Copper Blues aren’t just tenants—they’re experiences, and we’re proud to be their new home.”

Copper Blues Rock Pub & Kitchen, known for its fusion of American comfort food, craft cocktails, and live music has also been a popular spot for both locals and visitors, offering a vibrant atmosphere and diverse musical acts. Its relocation to Wellington aligns with the mall's initiative to evolve into a comprehensive lifestyle destination.

The addition of these venues is part of a broader strategy to revitalize The Mall at Wellington Green, enhancing its appeal as a hub for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

The mall is located at 10300 W. Forest Hill Blvd., Wellington. For more information, call (561) 227-6900 or visit shopwellingtongreen.com
