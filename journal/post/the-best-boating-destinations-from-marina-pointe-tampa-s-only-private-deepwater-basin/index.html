The best boating destinations from Marina Pointe, Tampa's only private deepwater basin

In the heart of Tampa's 52-acre idyllic Westshore Marina District, Marina Pointe continues to redefine luxury waterfront living. With...

By Jake Nicholas November 5, 2024

In the heart of Tampa's 52-acre idyllic Westshore Marina District, Marina Pointe continues to redefine luxury waterfront living. With Tower I complete, and Towers II and III coming soon, this exclusive community is capturing the attention of discerning buyers seeking an unparalleled lifestyle.

Marina Pointe Residences offers more than just luxurious living; it's your personal launchpad to explore the breathtaking beauty of Tampa Bay. From secluded beaches to vibrant cityscapes, this exclusive community provides unparalleled access to some of the region's most sought-after destinations.

Egmont Key State Park is a historic island located approximately 24.7 nautical miles away by boat. This pristine destination offers beautiful beaches, a lighthouse, and abundant wildlife.

Caladesi Island State Park is another popular choice situated approximately 40 nautical miles from St. Petersburg. Known for its white sand beaches and clear waters, this island is a haven for nature enthusiasts.

Anna Maria Island is a barrier island located approximately 31.4 nautical miles by boat. This tranquil destination offers pristine beaches, charming towns, and a laid-back atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, shelling, exploring the historic Anna Maria City Pier, shopping, dining, and discovering nearby nature preserves.

Beer Can Island: A secluded oasis in Tampa Bay, accessible only by boat or kayak and only about 14 nautical miles from Marina Pointe. This unique island is a popular destination for locals and visitors alike, known for its pristine waters, abundant marine life, and, as the name suggests, a colorful history. Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, fishing, or simply relaxing on the sandy shores while taking in the breathtaking views of the bay.
