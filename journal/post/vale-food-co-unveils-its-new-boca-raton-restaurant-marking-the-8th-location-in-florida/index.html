Vale Food Co unveils its new Boca Raton restaurant marking the 8th location in Florida

By Jake Nicholas September 18, 2023

Vale Food Co has unveiled its eighth location situated in Boca Raton. The brand emphasizes core principles like health, quality food, sustainability, and community involvement. Specializing in health-conscious dining, Vale Food Co offers a variety of protein, açai, and poke bowls, accompanied by a diverse range of beverages and bakery items.

Spanning 3,000 square feet, the Boca Raton location features a contemporary design infused with earthy tones and modern furniture. The indoor dining space accommodates 25 seats and is adorned with marble tables set against a palette of gold, black, green, and tropical hues. The design is consistent with other Vale locations, featuring an array of plants and greenery that underscore the brand's commitment to sustainability. An eye-catching, hand-painted mural on one of the walls encapsulates the essence of Vale’s ethos, “to live an earthly life.” Guests can also enjoy the outdoor patio, that offers a welcoming atmosphere ideal for various dining experiences.

The story of Vale Food Co began in 2014 when Founder and CEO Sunny Ilyas initially launched it as a meal delivery service targeting college students. Recognizing a market demand, Ilyas transitioned the brand into a fast-casual restaurant concept, opening the first location in Tallahassee in 2016. Subsequent locations in Gainesville, Tampa, Jacksonville, Fort Lauderdale, a second Tallahassee outpost, and most recently, Brickell, followed suit. Recently, Ilyas was named one of QSR's Young Restaurant Leaders to Watch and continues to steer the brand's evolution, from menu development to catering services.

“After much success with our locations throughout Florida and with a growing demand for healthy food, I’m excited to open our eighth Vale Food Co restaurant in the heart of Boca Raton and offer the community an all-day healthy dining destination like no other,” said Sunny Ilyas, CEO and Founder of Vale Food Co. “I’m confident that our fresh-forward food offerings and all-day vibe will cater to the diverse needs of Boca's dynamic neighborhood of families, children, and adults alike.”

The menu at Vale Food Co is designed for all-day dining and appeals to a broad audience, including families and children. The cold 'superfood' station features dishes like açai bowls, poke bowls, and avocado toasts, while the 'hot food' station offers customizable protein bowls and signature salads. Locally sourced ingredients make up the menu, which rotates seasonally and includes vegan and gluten-free options.

Vale Food Co is located at 5250 Town Center Circle #131, Boca Raton, FL 33486. The restaurant operates daily from 9 AM to 10 PM. For additional information, visit www.ValeFoodCo.com

Vale Food Co has plans for further expansion within Florida and other markets, and also offers catering services for large groups and private events.
