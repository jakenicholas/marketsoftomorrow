The Nashville EDITION: Luxury Hotel Glamour Meets High-Rise Living in The Gulch

By Jake Nicholas March 27, 2025

The Nashville skyline will soon add a radiant new addition with The Nashville EDITION, a 28-story tower that marries a five-star hotel and ultra-luxury residences. Tidal Real Estate Partners has officially unveiled a pre-sale website for The Residences at The Nashville EDITION, offering an early glimpse into this first-of-its-kind development ahead of a sales launch later in the spring.

Rising in the heart of The Gulch—across from Asurion’s headquarters at 1110 Porter Street​—the EDITION marks the Tennessee debut of the globally renowned EDITION Hotels brand. For luxury buyers, real estate investors, and engaged locals alike, this project represents a bold new chapter in Nashville’s evolution, blending the city’s Southern charm with modern “urban resort” living.

The Gulch has quickly become Nashville’s epicenter of upscale urban living - developed by Tidal Real Estate Partners in partnership with Marriott’s EDITION brand, the tower will stand 28 stories tall, adding a striking contemporary silhouette to the district’s skyline.

This first-to-market brand for Nashville is “celebrated for its sophisticated world-class hotels and record-setting residences in New York, West Hollywood, Miami Beach and other top destinations worldwide”, and its arrival will further elevate Nashville’s thriving hospitality culture.

With a newly launched website, official sales for the residences will commence later this spring​. Pricing is expected to start around $1.6 million for the private homes. Corcoran Sunshine Marketing Group, in tandem with local brokerage Corcoran Reverie, will be leading the charge​.

Occupying the top 15 floors of the tower, The Residences at The Nashville EDITION represent a curated collection of just 84 ultra-luxury homes perched above the hotel. These residences range from one to four bedrooms, each envisioned as a sanctuary in the sky with floor-to-ceiling views and refined finishes. At the very pinnacle sit two half-floor penthouse units, which will command the building’s uppermost levels and feature sweeping vistas of Downtown and Midtown Nashville​.

Design is a defining element of the EDITION brand, and the Nashville project is no exception. The tower’s architecture is helmed by ESa (Earl Swensson Associates), a Nashville-based firm known for shaping the city’s most notable buildings​. ESa’s contemporary design for the EDITION features a sleek glass façade and striking geometric forms at the base, ensuring the building is both modern and contextually tuned to its Gulch surroundings. Inside, INC Architecture & Design, an award-winning New York design studio, is crafting the interiors​. Renderings have been made by DBOX.

Owners of these residences will enjoy an amenity package on par with the world’s finest resorts. In addition to having priority access to all the services of the Nashville EDITION hotel – from in-home dining (private room service delivered to your door) to preferred reservations at its restaurants and spa – residents also have their own exclusive facilities​. Plans include a private fitness center outfitted with top-tier equipment and perhaps personal trainers on call, as well as a state-of-the-art golf simulator room​.

For relaxation, an outdoor pool deck with luxury cabanas will be available, as well as The EDITION's full-service spa. Indoors, multiple residents-only lounges and a private dining room are being designed as an extension of one’s home.

Equally exciting for Nashville is what’s happening below the residential floors: the EDITION hotel itself. The Nashville EDITION hotel will occupy the lower floors of the tower, and though many details are still unannounced, according to the developer’s plans, the hotel will feature approximately 282 guest rooms and an array of food and beverage venues – including a signature three-meal restaurant, a stylish lobby lounge, a rooftop bar with skyline views, a chic poolside bar, and potentially a 5,200-square-foot entertainment venue likely to host live music or special events​.

To understand what EDITION will bring to Nashville, consider its pedigree. EDITION Hotels is a collaboration between boutique hotel legend Ian Schrager (of Studio 54 and Delano fame) and hospitality giant Marriott International. Since its launch, the EDITION brand has carved out a niche at the intersection of luxury and lifestyle. Each property in the EDITION portfolio is unique to its location – whether it’s Miami Beach, with its famous tropical vibe and a Jean-Georges Vongerichten–helmed restaurant, or West Hollywood, with its glamorous rooftop and see-and-be-seen pool deck. Yet, all EDITION hotels share common threads: cutting-edge design, high-energy social spaces, top-tier dining and nightlife, and service that is as anticipatory as it is personalized.
