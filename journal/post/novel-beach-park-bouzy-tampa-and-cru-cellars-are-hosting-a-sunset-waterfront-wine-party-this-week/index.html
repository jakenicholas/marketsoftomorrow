NOVEL Beach Park, Bouzy Tampa, and Cru Cellars are hosting a Sunset Waterfront Wine Party this week

Experience an unforgettable evening of elegance and luxury at the Sunset Waterfront Wine Party hosted by NOVEL Beach Park in partnership...

By Jake Nicholas June 17, 2024

Experience an unforgettable evening of elegance and luxury at the Sunset Waterfront Wine Party hosted by NOVEL Beach Park in partnership with Bouzy Tampa and Cru Cellars. This exclusive event promises a delightful wine-tasting experience against the backdrop of the stunning Tampa Bay sunset.

Event Details:

Date: Friday, June 21st

Time: 7:00 PM - 9:30 PM

Price: $99 per person (Limited Spots Available)

Highlights:

  • Taste over 20 wines expertly paired with small bites from Cru Cellars and Bouzy.

  • Enjoy signature cocktails from a dedicated cocktail station.

  • Revel in the beauty of the summer sunset over Tampa Bay.

Tickets: https://www.crucellars.com/event/p/sunset-waterfront-wine-party-621

About NOVEL Beach Park

Nestled in the heart of Beach Park, NOVEL Beach Park is a brand-new waterfront community that offers an unparalleled living experience. This luxurious development features a range of studio, one, two, and three-bedroom apartments, along with opulent penthouses. Residents enjoy access to top-tier amenities that cater to both relaxation and an active lifestyle.

Special VIP Tours: During the event, take advantage of special VIP tours of NOVEL Beach Park, showcasing the elegant living spaces and premium amenities. Limited-time leasing offers will be available exclusively to event attendees.

Amenities at NOVEL Beach Park

Waterside Pool & Lounge:

  • Outdoor kitchens with multiple grilling areas.

  • A poolside lounge featuring a pool table, wet bar, spacious dining area, and two large flat-screen TVs.

  • Infinity-edge saltwater pool with social seating, sun chairs, and cabanas overlooking Old Tampa Bay.

Sunset Lounge:

  • Panoramic views of Old Tampa Bay, Downtown Tampa, Bayshore Blvd., and Downtown St. Petersburg.

  • Indoor/outdoor terrace with moveable accordion doors.

  • Rotating complimentary self-serve wine by the glass.

  • Media room with high-speed WiFi and a private dining area with a demonstration kitchen.

Co-Working Lounge:

  • Various spaces for remote work, including communal booths and a large conference room with screen-sharing capabilities.

  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi, office essentials, tea, coffee, and snacks.

Fitness Club:

  • 24-hour access to state-of-the-art Technogym fitness equipment.

  • Complimentary aromatherapy cooling towels.

  • Private yoga studio and spin room featuring LuLuLemon Fitness Mirrors and Peloton Bikes.

Clubroom:

  • Communal room with dining seating, catering table, and complimentary self-serve wine by the glass.

  • Card room complete with poker table and a variety of board games.

Gear Lounge:

  • Complimentary rental of bicycles, stand-up paddleboards, kayaks, and more through the resident-access Brevvie machine.

Pet-Friendly Perks:

  • Pet spa with grooming stalls, complimentary pet-care products, and an outdoor pet park with artificial turf and covered seating.

Convenience & Connectivity:

  • ButterflyMX Virtual entry systems, spacious covered parking with EV charging stations, valet trash services, and easy in-and-out bike storage.

  • Connected pathways to Beach Park, multiple elevators, and Fetch package delivery program.

  • Resident experience app and SmartRent technology package featuring keyless unit entry, Honeywell programmable thermostat and smart switches.

Resident Experiences:

  • Curated weekly and monthly events, local Tampa business partnerships with resident perks and discounts, and a dedicated on-site community ambassador.

Currently, NOVEL Beach Park is 25% occupied and 42% leased. Don’t miss the opportunity to be part of this exclusive community.

For more information and to secure your spot at the Sunset Waterfront Wine Party, visit NOVEL Beach Park's website or contact our leasing team directly.
