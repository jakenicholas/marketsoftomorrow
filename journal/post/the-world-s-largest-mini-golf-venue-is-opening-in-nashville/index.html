The world’s largest mini-golf venue is opening in Nashville

PopStroke is opening the largest mini-golf destination in the world December 28th at Century Farms in Antioch.

By Jake Nicholas December 23, 2025

PopStroke is officially bringing its biggest, boldest concept yet to Music City. December 28th, 2025 PopStroke Entertainment Group will open PopStroke Nashville at Century Farms in Antioch, marking the brand’s first Tennessee location—and its largest venue anywhere in the world.

Founded by Greg Bartoli in partnership with Tiger Woods and TaylorMade Golf Company, the Nashville opening becomes PopStroke’s 20th location nationwide and its seventh state. Designed as a full-scale eatertainment destination, the venue blends next-level putting, chef-driven dining, and all-weather social spaces into one massive, music-fueled playground.

At the heart of the experience are three 18-hole putting courses built with championship-style synthetic turf, surrounded by indoor and outdoor games, five massive jumbotrons, a playground, and a two-story restaurant and bar. The result feels part sports bar, part mini-golf arena—equally suited for date nights, family outings, corporate events, and game-day crowds.

The culinary anchor is BarTenders, PopStroke’s elevated restaurant concept featuring a scratch kitchen and reimagined comfort food, from hand-breaded chicken tenders to ahi tuna nachos. The bar program spotlights local craft beers, seasonal cocktails, dirty sodas, and mocktails, while the on-site ice cream parlor rounds things out with 24 flavors of Blue Bell, hand-spun milkshakes, and classic soda floats.

PopStroke Nashville also debuts the company’s first dual-climate design, with roughly 75,000 square feet of indoor space paired with 25,000 square feet of outdoor putting greens, allowing guests to move seamlessly between open-air patios and indoor lounges year-round.

“Nashville is an incredibly dynamic city and a perfect fit for our first Tennessee location,” said Greg Bartoli. “PopStroke is just as much about great food and social energy as it is about golf—and this venue is designed for people to come for dinner and drinks, then stay for the putting, the music, and the atmosphere.”

Membership options will offer unlimited golf with no blackout dates, food and beverage discounts, and access to leagues, events, and exclusive programming—cementing PopStroke Nashville as a repeat-visit destination, not just a one-time stop.

The December opening adds another major entertainment anchor to the fast-growing Century Farms district and introduces a new kind of large-scale, culinary-driven mini-golf experience to the greater Nashville area.
