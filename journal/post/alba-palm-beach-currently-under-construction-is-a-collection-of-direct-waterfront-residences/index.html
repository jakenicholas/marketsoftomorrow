Alba Palm Beach, currently under construction, is a collection of direct waterfront residences

Nestled in a serene West Palm Beach neighborhood, Alba Palm Beach offers an exclusive escape where modern elegance meets coastal...

By Jake Nicholas October 15, 2024

Nestled in a serene West Palm Beach neighborhood, Alba Palm Beach offers an exclusive escape where modern elegance meets coastal tranquility. Just steps from the Intracoastal Waterway and a short drive from the pristine beaches, Worth Avenue's upscale boutiques, and vibrant Atlantic Ocean boating scene, this intimate community presents a refined waterfront lifestyle. Experience the best of both worlds: the seclusion of a quiet neighborhood and the convenience of world-class amenities, all within reach.

Rendering Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach

Alba Palm Beach recently unveiled new renderings, exclusively on Florida of Tomorrow, of its lower penthouse and residence C and D floor plans. These residences feature expansive outdoor spaces overlooking the Intracoastal, complete with stylish furniture by Artefacto. Buyers at Alba Palm Beach have the unique opportunity to purchase these curated furniture packages, offering a truly turnkey lifestyle.

Renderings Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach, a Florida of Tomorrow Premiere

Of the new 6,600 luxury condo units across West Palm Beach, Alba is one of the leaders, on track for completion in Spring 2026. Units start at just under $3 million, and approximately 50% of the development has already been sold.

Renderings Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach, a Florida of Tomorrow Premiere

Designed by renowned architects Spina O’Rourke + Partners, Alba Palm Beach offers a collection of 55 boutique residences, including two- and three-bedroom floor plans and exclusive two- and three-story townhomes.

Renderings Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach, a Florida of Tomorrow Premiere

These residences boast luxurious features such as Italian-designed indoor and outdoor kitchens by Italkraft, top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances from SubZero® and Wolf®, and floor-to-ceiling hurricane impact windows.

Residents of Alba Palm Beach will enjoy a world-class array of amenities. From sunrise and sunset pools with private cabanas to art and relaxation gardens, a multi-sport simulator, a state-of-the-art Technogym with water views, and a luxurious spa featuring a his/her sauna, steam room, and cold shower, every detail has been thoughtfully considered.

Rendering Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach

For culinary enthusiasts, Alba Palm Beach offers a private dining room for Chef-led dining experiences and personal climate-controlled wine storage.

Renderings Courtesy of Alba Palm Beach, a Florida of Tomorrow Premiere

Beyond the property's walls, residents will have exclusive access to the best of West Palm Beach. Enjoy membership benefits to the iconic Palm Beach Yacht Club and the prestigious New York Mount Sinai Health System’s Concierge Medicine. Alba Palm Beach offers a truly exceptional lifestyle, combining luxury, convenience, and breathtaking waterfront views.
