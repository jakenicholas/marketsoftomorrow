Lefferts launch new luxury 14-story condominium tower, PALMA Miami Beach Residences, offering 126 turnkey residences starting at $650,000

Lefferts, a developer renowned for high-end residential and commercial properties, has announced the upcoming PALMA Miami Beach...

By Jake Nicholas April 24, 2024

Lefferts, a developer renowned for high-end residential and commercial properties, has announced the upcoming PALMA Miami Beach Residences, a luxury condominium tower located at 600 71st Street in Miami Beach. The 14-story building will offer 126 turnkey residences, fully furnished, ranging from 405 to 889 square feet, and is expected to open in early 2026 with prices starting from the $650,000s. This LEED Gold-certified property is positioned within the pedestrian-friendly North Beach neighborhood, close to recreational and cultural amenities like the Miami Beach Tennis Academy, North Shore Park Youth Center, and Normandy Shores Golf Club.

The development will feature a blend of one- and two-bedroom units along with seven penthouse suites, each boasting private balconies, high ceilings, and luxurious fittings such as European cabinetry and Bosch appliances in the kitchens, and sleek bathroom fixtures. Residents will enjoy 10,000 square feet of communal spaces including a resort-style pool, a wellness hot tub, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a media and game lounge. The project is designed by national architecture firm Built Form, with interiors by Studio Ramirez reflecting South Florida’s lush landscape and Miami Beach influences.

Lefferts continues to expand its portfolio in the North Beach area, known for its Miami Modernist architecture and extensive green spaces. The firm's ongoing projects in the neighborhood include the 72 Park luxury residential tower and the newly approved 72 & Carlyle luxury condominium. Peggy Olin, CEO of OneWorld Properties, expressed enthusiasm about partnering with Lefferts for the sales and marketing of PALMA, highlighting the project's alignment with the firm's experience in serving international buyers and flexible ownership models.

For more information on PALMA Miami Beach Residences and to explore the vibrant, revitalized North Beach community, visit PalmaMiamiBeach.com
