400 Channelside, a 19-story development in Water Street Tampa will feature a 30,000 sqft sky garden

The 400 Channelside building, situated in Water Street Tampa, is designed as a hub for urban engagement, acting as a gateway to the...

By Jake Nicholas October 13, 2023

The 400 Channelside building, situated in Water Street Tampa, is designed as a hub for urban engagement, acting as a gateway to the thriving waterfront district of Tampa. This 19-story mixed-use edifice encompasses 500,000 square feet of Class A office space, along with 110,000 square feet of retail space and a 27,000 square-foot rooftop terrace. The building is crafted by the global architecture firm Gensler, and is conceived to fulfill the diverse needs of tenants, ranging from small to multi-floor companies with varying floor plans to accommodate all.

One of the unique features of 400 Channelside is its dedication to wellness and environmental sustainability. The design encourages a healthy lifestyle by integrating indoor and outdoor spaces, which not only enhances mental and physical wellness but also emphasizes environmental sustainability and air quality. A notable feature is the 27,000 square foot rooftop terrace located on the fourth floor, which provides a multifaceted outdoor space for fitness, informal gatherings, business meetings, or quiet rejuvenation.

Another distinctive design element is the "sky garden," which spans 30,000 square feet and resides on the fourth floor, offering a variety of outdoor spaces for meetings, events, fitness activities, and informal gatherings, thus connecting the building occupants to the outdoors in a refreshing manner.

This development is part of a broader urban revitalization effort in the Water Street Tampa area, which is seeing a mix of residential, commercial, and recreational spaces coming up, contributing to the overall urban fabric and making the district a vibrant place for both work and leisure.
