The Palm Beaches

Some standout residential offerings in and around West Palm Beach

Palm Beach County is experiencing a wave of luxury residential developments that are redefining modern living with stunning architecture,...

By Jake Nicholas January 16, 2025

Palm Beach County is experiencing a wave of luxury residential developments that are redefining modern living with stunning architecture, exclusive amenities, and breathtaking waterfront locations. From sleek loft residences to grand estate homes and boutique condominiums, these projects showcase a perfect blend of innovation, elegance, and the unparalleled beauty of South Florida. Here’s a closer look at eight extraordinary developments—each offering a unique take on luxury, designed to elevate the way residents live, relax, and enjoy this coveted destination.

Panther National

With an extraordinary vision of luxury and sustainability, Panther National is redefining golf community living. Developed under the vision of Founder and President Dominik Senn, their signature Max Strang-designed Clubhouse is set to debut in Fall 2026, offering an array of world-class amenities that redefine luxury living.

This exclusive enclave in Palm Beach Gardens features custom estate homes designed by world-renowned architects and builders, blending modern aesthetics with eco-conscious principles. The community is anchored by a Jack Nicklaus & Justin Thomas Signature golf course, catering to the modern buyer who values privacy, luxury, golf, and club living - making it one of the region’s most exciting residential developments.

Alba Palm Beach

Alba Palm Beach is an exclusive waterfront haven featuring just 55 residences, designed to deliver privacy, luxury, and sophistication. Each home boasts expansive terraces, floor-to-ceiling windows, and panoramic views of the Intracoastal Waterway and the Atlantic Ocean. Alba’s amenities rival those of a five-star resort, including a private dock for boating enthusiasts, a swimming pool overlooking the water, a fitness center with cutting-edge equipment, and lushly landscaped outdoor spaces for relaxation. With its boutique atmosphere and prime location near West Palm Beach’s quickly-growing downtown, Alba is a true gem for those who value serenity and elegance.

Haven Palm Beach

Nestled in the heart of Palm Beach, Haven is redefining urban living with its loft-style residences within steps of the waterfront and park. These luxury homes offer bring open floor plans, industrial-inspired elements, and oversized windows that flood the interiors with natural light and water views. Residents receive a gym membership to the next door The Ben, Autograph Hotel, as well as a JetRide membership for jet ski and boat rides. Perfectly situated, Haven provides unparalleled access to the area's vibrant dining, shopping, and cultural attractions, while also living in a building with not many neighbors.

The Berkeley Palm Beach

This upcoming 26-story waterfront condominium, developed by Al Adelson, the visionary behind The Bristol, is set to redefine living in downtown West Palm Beach. Situated along Clear Lake, The Berkeley offers 193 residences ranging from 2 to 5 bedrooms, each featuring expansive flow-through living spaces and some of the largest east and west-facing balconies in the area. Residents will enjoy unobstructed waterfront views and a suite of luxury amenities, including concierge services, valet parking, a state-of-the-art fitness center, spa facilities, and both family and rooftop pools with cabanas.

The Berkeley is designed to cater to modern families and professionals, providing innovative flex spaces adaptable to various lifestyles.

Mr. C Residences West Palm Beach

Inspired by timeless European sophistication, Mr. C Residences West Palm Beach brings the Cipriani family’s signature style to South Florida. This waterfront development blends old-world charm with modern convenience, offering impeccably designed residences that emphasize natural light, clean lines, and high-end finishes. Amenities include a poolside cabana oasis, a residents-only restaurant curated by Cipriani, a wellness center, as well as access to the hotel's amenities. Strategically designed to separate the residences and hotel guests, Mr. C Residences has seperate entracnces, elevators, pools and private spaces, ensuring residents' privacy.

South Flagler House

A masterpiece in architectural design, South Flagler House is poised to be the most luxurious condominium tower in the West Palm Beach skyline. Designed by Robert A.M. Stern Architects, the two-tower, 108-unit development showcases an iconic neoclassical style, with sweeping views of the Intracoastal, Palm Beach Island, and the Atlantic Ocean. Residences feature grand layouts with private terraces, bespoke finishes, and an unmatched level of detail. South Flagler House’s amenities, including a spa, a resort-style pool, private dining rooms, and a 24-hour concierge, set a new standard for ultra-luxury living in South Florida. With prices ranging from $5.9 million to $72.5 million, the project targets a high-end demographic, drawing significant interest from New Yorkers recently.

Nautilus 220

Located in Lake Park, Nautilus 220 is a modern mixed-use development offering 330 luxury residences complemented by retail and dining spaces. These waterfront homes feature contemporary designs with open layouts, gourmet kitchens, and terraces that frame breathtaking views of the marina and Intracoastal Waterway. Residents enjoy an array of resort-style amenities, including multiple pools, a wellness center, a private marina with yacht slips, and a waterfront restaurant. Nautilus 220 is perfect for those seeking a blend of luxury, convenience, and vibrant community living.

HAVN Residences & Yacht Club

Positioned as a sanctuary for boating enthusiasts, HAVN Residences & Yacht Club delivers an exclusive boutique waterfront lifestyle. These limited-edition 36 homes offer sleek, modern interiors, private boat slips, and expansive outdoor living spaces. Residents are immersed in a resort-like atmosphere, complete with a private marina, waterside pool, outdoor lounge areas, and personalized concierge services. HAVN is where nautical luxury meets contemporary elegance, offering a seamless blend of land and sea in one of Palm Beach County’s most coveted locales.

The Ritz-Carlton Residences, Palm Beach Gardens

The Ritz-Carlton Residences in Palm Beach Gardens offer the pinnacle of luxury living with a focus on waterfront elegance and unmatched service. This collection of homes is nestled along the Intracoastal Waterway, with private marina access and lushly landscaped grounds. Set on the last 14 acres of contiguous waterfront in Palm Beach, this exclusive development is now 40% sold and on track for completion in early 2026. The East and North Towers have reached their summits, while the South Tower surpasses 50% completion. The property offers 106 grand estate residences with breathtaking 180-degree views of the Intracoastal. Prices range from $4.5 million to $10 million, with a rare collection of 13 Grand Terrace Residences boasting up to 1,834 square feet of private terrace space leading to a 15,000-square-foot mezzanine amenity deck.
