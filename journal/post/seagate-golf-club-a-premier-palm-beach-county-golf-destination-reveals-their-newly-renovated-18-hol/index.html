Seagate Golf Club, a premier Palm Beach County golf destination reveals their newly renovated 18-hole championship course, led by world-renowned architect Drew Rogers

By Jake Nicholas April 30, 2024

The Seagate Golf Club, a premier destination in Palm Beach County, has reopened its 18-hole championship golf course following a comprehensive renovation led by world-renowned architect Drew Rogers. This revitalization enhances the Joe Lee-designed course, situated amidst 300 acres of lush Bimini Bermuda grass and tree-lined fairways, offering an unparalleled golfing experience.

The newly renovated course provides a complete range of facilities, including a fully-equipped driving range, pitching and bunker areas, and guidance from PGA professionals. The renovations, which led to enhancements and redesigns across all 18 holes, bring fully integrated improvements to greens, fairways, tees, bunkers, irrigation, drainage, landscaping, and the practice facilities. The result is a seamless blend of challenging gameplay and elegant design.

"Recognized as one of the best courses in the region, this renovation serves to continue providing our members with world-class facilities," said Alex Schnoeller, Managing Director of The Seagate. Alan Fike, Director of Agronomy, emphasized the detailed execution of the redesign, ensuring all intricate design details were realized to plan.

In addition to the course, the club has also revitalized its 41,064-square-foot Clubhouse, featuring a variety of dining options and event spaces, including a dining room, Grille Room, and Courtyard. The renovations bring a serene aesthetic inspired by Palm Beach's heritage, with soothing neutrals, sage greens, and historic colorways complemented by jute wallcovering and elegant upholstery. The refreshed space also features curated art, blending painterly abstracts with vintage photos of Palm Beach by Slim Aarons. The patio showcases marble dining tables and rattan furniture, reflecting a relaxed nod to Hollywood Regency glamour.

About The Seagate Golf Club

The Seagate Golf Club is a renowned golf and country club in Delray Beach, home to a newly renovated 18-hole championship golf course and practice facilities. The Club also offers a Racquets Center with programs for tennis and pickleball at every skill level, as well as a state-of-the-art fitness facility focusing on total mind/body wellness, featuring Technogym equipment, Kinesis stations, and personal training sessions. Dining options range from casual meals at the 19th Hole Café to formal dinners in the dining room. For more information about membership, including access to the Club's wellness, tennis, dining, and golf amenities, please visit seagatedelray.com/membership
