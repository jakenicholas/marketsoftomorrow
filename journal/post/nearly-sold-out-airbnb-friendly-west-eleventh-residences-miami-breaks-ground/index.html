Nearly sold out Airbnb-friendly West Eleventh Residences Miami breaks ground

National developer PMG , Lion Development Group , and Marc Roberts Companies proudly celebrate the groundbreaking of West Eleventh...

By Jake Nicholas September 25, 2024

National developer PMG, Lion Development Group, and Marc Roberts Companies proudly celebrate the groundbreaking of West Eleventh Residences Miami. The project is expected to rise 44 stories and offer 659 fully furnished luxury residences in Miami’s Park West neighborhood, along with short-term rental flexibility and an opportunity for owners to enjoy the ability to host guests 365 days a year on Airbnb*. 

Renderings: ARX Creative, West Eleventh Residences Miami

“West Eleventh Residences Miami provides an unmatched level of flexibility and convenience for prospective buyers to fit their unique needs. Whether year-round or seasonally, owners can expect to enjoy a premier location within Downtown Miami’s Park West neighborhood, which is quickly rising as the top entertainment district in the city, along with the property’s vibrant amenities and ground floor experience,” said Ryan Shear, Managing Partner at PMG. 

Located at 18 Northwest 11th Street, West Eleventh Residences Miami will bring an impressive array of amenities to the entertainment district’s vibrant atmosphere, including a distinctive 30,000 square foot, tri-level experiential entertainment venue. PMG Residential exclusively represents sales for West Eleventh Residences Miami. 

Renderings: ARX Creative, West Eleventh Residences Miami

Raquel Kaufman is the Director of Sales for the accomplished sales team: Adam Freeman, Reiner Perez, Liza Hernadez, Pablo Rodriguez, Helen Vargas, Carlos Guitierrez, Orissa Claver, and Reiner Perez Jr.  Each owner at West Eleventh Residences Miami will have the unique opportunity to host on Airbnb year-round, the first residential-condominium building in Miami to offer this feature.

“As Miami has continued to evolve as a destination for dining and entertainment, we’re seeing more individuals become enthralled with the world-class lifestyle our city offers,” said Michael Simkins, President and CEO of Lion Development Group.

Designed by internationally renowned architecture firm Sieger Suarez Architects, with residential interiors by Cotofana Designs, West Eleventh Residences Miami will offer both city living and access to nearby beaches. Each of its fully-finished and fully-furnished studio and one-bedroom units will also feature the latest smart building technology, and top-of-the-line appliances.

Renderings: ARX Creative, West Eleventh Residences Miami

At West Eleventh Residences Miami, residents can enjoy a luxurious lifestyle with over 20,000 square feet of amenities. Whether it’s relaxing by the expansive pool overlooking the Miami skyline, savoring culinary delights at the signature supper club restaurant on the main floor, or unwinding in private lounge areas, there is something for everyone.

Renderings: ARX Creative, West Eleventh Residences Miami

The state-of-the-art fitness centers, wellness features, and co-working space additionally ensure that residents will have access to every comfort and convenience right from home.

“Park West undoubtedly presents its residents with the best of every aspect of Miami, from year-round entertainment to business opportunities and one-of-a-kind experiences,” said Marc Roberts of Marc Roberts Companies.

For more information on West Eleventh Residences Miami, please call (786) 572-3444 or visit WestEleventhResidences.com

*Hosting is subject to local laws and other restrictions.
