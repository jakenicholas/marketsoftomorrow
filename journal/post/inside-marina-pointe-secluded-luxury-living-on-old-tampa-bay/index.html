Inside Marina Pointe, secluded luxury living on Old Tampa Bay

By Jake Nicholas April 17, 2025

Marina Pointe in Tampa’s prestigious Westshore Marina District epitomizes waterfront luxury living. Poised on a secluded peninsula along Old Tampa Bay, this upscale condominium enclave offers Tampa’s only private deepwater marina, panoramic bay views, and resort-caliber amenities. Every aspect of Marina Pointe’s design – from its modern coastal architecture to its lush pool decks – showcases a lifestyle of elegance by land and sea. With Tampa’s largest private marina at its doorstep and vibrant city attractions minutes away, Marina Pointe beckons those seeking the ultimate Florida waterfront lifestyle.

A Unique Location by Land and Sea

Nestled within the Westshore Marina District, Marina Pointe occupies a coveted location where urban convenience meets coastal tranquility. This 52-acre master-planned district spans 1.5 miles of scenic waterfront, parks, and green space. Residents enjoy a private oasis that feels tucked away, yet it’s remarkably central – just a short bridge from downtown St. Petersburg and a quick drive from downtown Tampa. The development sits on Tampa Bay’s most protected deepwater yacht basin, meaning calm waters and a safe harbor right at home. Each morning can start with the sun rising over the bay and unobstructed water views stretching toward the horizon. Here, waterfront living isn’t a distant dream; it’s an everyday reality, with breathtaking bay views visible from expansive balconies and floor-to-ceiling windows in many residences.

At the heart of Marina Pointe’s appeal is its 159-slip private marina, a feature that sets it apart in the Tampa Bay area. This state-of-the-art deepwater harbor accommodates yachts from 40 to 90 feet and offers a 15-foot channel depth. It’s a boater’s paradise: Residents have the rare privilege of stepping out of their luxury condo and onto a boat for a day of sailing, fishing, or cruising the Gulf. “The marina has become a central part of the community,” notes Marina Pointe’s Executive Director Dominic Pickering, calling it “a significant differentiator” in the luxury condo market. Even residents without boats relish living next to the marina – people love to have views of water in all directions, Pickering says, and the opportunity to join neighbors for a sunset cruise “adds a special dimension to the lifestyle”. In other words, the marina isn’t just a place to dock; it’s a social and scenic extension of home.

Exclusive Amenities and Elegant Design

Beyond the mesmerizing water views, Marina Pointe pampers residents with a suite of resort-style amenities designed for comfort and indulgence. The twin palm-fringed pool decks create a private resort atmosphere, each with a heated waterside pool overlooking the bay. 

Residents can lounge in daybeds beneath trellises, unwind in a secluded heated spa, or enjoy friendly competition on the waterfront bocce court. For fitness and wellness, a state-of-the-art fitness center comes complete with the latest cardio and strength equipment, a yoga zen garden, and spa-like locker rooms with steam and massage rooms. There’s even a virtual golf suite, allowing residents to tee off on famed courses via high-tech simulators.

Indoor luxuries continue with elegant social lounges and a 24-hour concierge to cater to residents’ needs. In Marina Pointe’s private clubrooms, neighbors gather for wine tastings and casual get-togethers, fostering a close-knit community spirit. Every tower lobby is attended by a friendly concierge around the clock, epitomizing the development’s commitment to service and security. The residences themselves feature modern open layouts with floor-to-ceiling glass walls and oversized terraces, so each home frames the tranquil bay panoramas and city skyline in stunning detail. From gourmet kitchens to spa-like owner’s baths, the interior finishes reflect a dedication to high design and comfort.

Some of Marina Pointe’s standout amenities include:

  • Private Deepwater Marina: 159 resident-only slips with a dockmaster, full utilities, and 24/7 security – offering direct access to Tampa Bay and the Gulf beyond.

  • Resort-Style Pools & Spa: Two expansive pool decks with heated pools, lap lanes, sun shelves, and a jacuzzi spa, all overlooking Old Tampa Bay’s sparkling waters.

  • Fitness & Wellness Center: 3 cutting-edge gyms, yoga lawn, steam rooms, and massage studio that make wellness a daily pleasure.

  • Entertainment & Leisure: 3 residents’ lounges with catering kitchen and bar, virtual golf simulator room, bocce ball court, and even a putting green for outdoor fun.

  • Concierge Services: 24/7 concierge staffing, an on-site manager, and curated social events (wine tastings, coffee mornings, paddleboard outings) that elevate living into an effortless luxury experience.

Each element of Marina Pointe’s amenity package is thoughtfully curated to blend coastal relaxation with city sophistication, ensuring residents feel like they’re on a permanent vacation at home.

Vibrant Westshore Marina District Lifestyle

Part of Marina Pointe’s allure is its location within the Westshore Marina District, a walkable waterfront neighborhood buzzing with dining, shopping, and recreation. Just outside the condominium towers lies Marina Landings Town Center, with trendy boutiques, cafes, and gourmet restaurants. The pedestrian-friendly design encourages biking or walking along pathways that wind past the marina and through Sunset Park’s lush greenery. This seamless integration of nature and urban amenities means you can launch a paddleboard at the private dock in the morning or a farmers’ market in the afternoon without leaving the neighborhood.

Marina Pointe’s strategic perch on Tampa’s western shore also means unrestricted access to open-water adventures. Boating enthusiasts love being able to cruise to local favorites like St. Petersburg, picnic on the sands of Beer Can Island, or dock at waterside venues like Sparkman Wharf for a night out Whether it’s fishing for snook at dawn or enjoying a champagne sunset aboard a neighbor’s yacht, the experiences available to Marina Pointe residents embody Tampa Bay’s outdoor-loving, cosmopolitan vibe. And when city pursuits call, Tampa’s thriving downtown – with its museums, sports arenas, and performing arts centers – is just a 15-minute drive away. This rare combination of serene coastal retreat and city proximity positions Marina Pointe in a class of its own.

Life at Marina Pointe: In Tower I Residents’ Words

The lifestyle at Marina Pointe is more than just luxury marketing – it’s a daily reality cherished by those who call it home. “There’s nothing like waking up... at 5:30 in the morning, hitting the elevator and going downstairs,” says resident Cliffe Laborde, who moved from Louisiana in search of the perfect condo-and-boat-slip combination. With his 52-foot cruiser moored in Marina Pointe’s harbor, Cliffe enjoys spontaneous morning fishing trips in Tampa Bay: “We’ll run 15 minutes from here and slay the snook and redfish... It’s unbelievable,” he enthuses, describing the thrill of casting off at dawn and being back by late morning with a fresh catch. For Cliffe and neighbors like him, the development’s promise of an easy lock-and-leave boating lifestyle has delivered in full.

Developers BTI Partners anticipated this passion for the water. “If there are boat slips, buy one. You won’t regret it,” was the advice Cliffe received – wisdom he’s glad he heeded, as the demand for slips is soaring. Even beyond the marina, residents appreciate how Marina Pointe fosters community. Many share that their favorite evenings are spent on the amenity deck or at dockside socials, watching the sunset paint the sky while chatting with neighbors who have become friends. The combination of friendly gatherings and a like-minded love for the water creates a warm, inviting atmosphere not always found in high-rise living. From the attentive concierge staff who know residents by name to the occasional group paddleboard outing, Marina Pointe offers not just a home, but a true lifestyle community.

The Pinnacle of Tampa Bay Living

In every sense, Marina Pointe captures what makes Tampa Bay one of Florida’s most desirable locales for luxury living. It’s a place where modern high-rise comforts meet the simple pleasures of sun and sea – where you can gaze at downtown skyline lights from your balcony at night and awaken to dolphins playing in the bay at dawn. The informative yet inviting tone of Marina Pointe’s community speaks to discerning buyers who seek more than an address; they seek an experience. Here, that experience might include weekend brunch at an award-winning waterfront restaurant a short walk away, a relaxing afternoon by an infinity-edge pool, or a sunset cruise launched from your own backyard marina.

Marina Pointe stands as a testament to Tampa’s evolution into a luxury waterfront destination, blending natural beauty with urban sophistication. Its exclusive water views, private boat slips, and elegant amenities set a new standard for coastal living in the region. For potential buyers, the message is clear: if you’re looking to embrace the best of Tampa’s blue-water lifestyle – from the thrill of boating adventures to tranquil evenings in a stylish sanctuary – Marina Pointe is the place to drop anchor and truly “set sail for a new standard of luxury”. The beauty and lifestyle of Marina Pointe offer an unparalleled invitation to live the good life on Tampa’s waterfront, every single day.
