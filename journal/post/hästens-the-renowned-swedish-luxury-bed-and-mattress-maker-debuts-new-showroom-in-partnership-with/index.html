The Palm Beaches

Hästens, the renowned Swedish luxury bed and mattress maker debuts new showroom in partnership with Hive Collective

Hästens , the renowned Swedish luxury bed and mattress maker, is thrilled to announce the grand opening of its newest showroom in Palm...

By Jake Nicholas March 18, 2025

Hästens, the renowned Swedish luxury bed and mattress maker, is thrilled to announce the grand opening of its newest showroom in Palm Beach, located at 1817 S. Dixie Highway, West Palm Beach, FL 33401. This exciting venture is in partnership with Sara McCann's acclaimed Hive Collective, marking a significant milestone in bringing the brand’s unparalleled craftsmanship and heritage to discerning customers in South Florida.

The new Hästens 2,900-square-foot showroom offers an immersive experience that invites visitors to explore the art of sleep. Known for its six generations of master craftsmanship, Hästens combines traditional techniques with the finest all-natural materials to create beds that epitomize luxury, comfort, and sustainability. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted in Sweden, embodying over 170 years of expertise.

“We are delighted to open our doors in Palm Beach, a community that appreciates quality, craftsmanship, and design,” said Sara McCann, owner of Hive Collective. “This showroom is not just a retail space; it’s a destination where customers can truly understand the transformative power of a Hästens bed.”

Visitors to the showroom will have the opportunity to test a curated selection of Hästens’ iconic products (prices begin at $26,000), including the legendary Grand Vividus (beginning at $658,000), the world’s most luxurious and masterfully crafted bed. The space has been thoughtfully designed to reflect Hästens’ Scandinavian roots and Hive Collective’s sophisticated and refined aesthetic, with an emphasis on serenity and timeless elegance.

Located next to Hive Bed & Bath, Hive Collective’s newest destination for luxurious bedding, bedroom furniture, bath linens, and essentials, Hästens Palm Beach offers customers a complementary experience. Together, the two spaces create a one-stop destination for those seeking to elevate their home and sleep environment.

The opening of the Palm Beach showroom represents Hästens’ continued growth and commitment to providing world-class sleep solutions to customers across the globe. By partnering with Hive Collective, the brand underscores its dedication to aligning with like-minded businesses that share a passion for excellence and design.

Grand Opening Details

A grand opening event is scheduled for April—stay tuned at @Hastens_PalmBeach for more details. In the meantime, customers are invited to visit the showroom during regular business hours, Monday through Saturday from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m., to discover how a Hästens bed can revolutionize their sleep and well-being. Appointments can also be booked online via the Hästens.com Store Locator or by phone at (561) 559-1990. 

Location: Hästens Showroom, Palm Beach - 1817 S. Dixie Highway, West Palm Beach, FL 33401

About Hästens

Founded in 1852 in Köping, Sweden, Hästens is a world leader in handcrafted beds and mattresses. Renowned for its dedication to quality, sustainability, and innovation, the brand continues to set the global standard for luxurious sleep. Hästens beds are made using natural materials such as horsehair, cotton, wool, and flax, ensuring ultimate comfort and longevity.

About Hive Collective

Hive Collective, led by Sara McCann, is a celebrated name in interior design and lifestyle curation. Based in Palm Beach, Hive Collective encompasses a family of brands devoted to luxury products and high-quality services, including McCann Design Group; Hive Home, Gift & Garden; Hive for Her, Him & Kids; Hive Bakery & Café; Hive Trade Showroom; Hickory Chair Palm Beach; Hive Bed & Bath; Pure by Hive; and Hive for Yachts.
