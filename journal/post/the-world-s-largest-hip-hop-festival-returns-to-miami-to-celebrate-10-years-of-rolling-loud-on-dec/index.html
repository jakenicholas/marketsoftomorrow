The World’s Largest Hip-Hop Festival Returns to Miami to Celebrate “10 Years of Rolling Loud” on December 13-15 at Hard Rock Stadium

Continuing to push boundaries as it celebrates its 10th year as a culture-shifting force, Rolling Loud always does it biggest in its...

By Jake Nicholas August 13, 2024

Continuing to push boundaries as it celebrates its 10th year as a culture-shifting force, Rolling Loud always does it biggest in its hometown. Today, the world’s largest hip-hop festival announces the lineup for Rolling Loud Miami, its flagship festival. Recruiting dozens of rap’s most exciting stars for the largest “10 Years of Rolling Loud” celebration yet, Rolling Loud Miami 2024 will be headlined by Travis Scott, Playboi Carti, and Future. Featuring one of its most impressive lineups to date, Rolling Loud Miami returns home December 13-15th at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens, FL. GA and VIP tickets for Rolling Loud Miami 2024 are available HERE on August 16 at 12pm ET.

A fixture at Rolling Loud since the first Miami festival in 2015, Travis Scott is one of his generation’s defining rap superstars, with a stage presence and spectacle that has grown along with Rolling Loud over the past decade. Playboi Carti returns to Rolling Loud, headlining Miami for the second straight year, as he represents for the ascendant internet underground. 2024’s hottest rapper-producer duo, Future returns after their show-stopping performance at Rolling Loud California 2024 with Metro Boomin, which featured high-wattage special guests and the first-ever performance of “Like That,” the duo’s chart-topping collaboration with Kendrick Lamar, kicking off the biggest storyline in hip-hop in 2024.

"In four and a half years — beginning small in Miami, and now promoting Coachella-sized events around the country — Rolling Loud has established itself as a playground for the young...This time last year, many of the rappers now drawing crowds of several thousand were barely known." - The New York Times

Beyond the A-List headliners, Rolling Loud Miami promises a stacked undercard that reflects the landscape of modern hip-hop. Fans at Hard Rock can expect sizzling sets from superstars like Don Toliver, Polo G, Bryson Tiller, NLE Choppa, Yeat, and Lil Yachty, leading ladies like Sexyy Red, JT, TiaCorine, Lay Bankz, Bktherula, and Anycia, Florida-born favorites like Kodak Black, Ski Mask The Slump God, Rick Ross, BossMan Dlow, Real Boston Richey, and BLP Kosher, and promising risers like Ken Carson, Cash Cobain, Eem Triplin, Skaiwater, 4Batz, Osamason, Lazer Dim 700, and many more. Some of the veterans performing at Miami 2024 have been Rolling Loud regulars since the first Miami show in 2015, including Curren$y, LUCKI, Denzel Curry, and Robb Bank$. For the first time since Billy Ray Cyrus’s memorable appearance with Lil Nas X in 2019, Rolling Loud Miami welcomes crossover country artists, highlighted by #1 hitmaker Shaboozey and rising diva Reyna Roberts. As always, fans can expect Rolling Loud Miami to bring a diverse complement of surprises and special guests into the fold.

Rolling Loud is partnering with Brightline to provide the ultimate ride to the world's biggest hip-hop festival. Brightline, the USA's only private intercity railroad, connects people to the hottest experiences in Florida, and is offering Rolling Loud fans a seamless journey with complimentary shuttle service to the festival from Brightline Aventura Station, free with the purchase of a Brightline train ticket. Fans can check for the Hard Rock Stadium Connect icon when booking a trip at www.gobrightline.com, and can save 25% on SMART fares or groups of 3 or more.

Throughout 2024, Rolling Loud commemorates "10 Years Of Rolling Loud," celebrating the festival's rise from humble origins to world dominance. Since the first Miami festival, Rolling Loud has evolved into a major force in hip-hop, providing a massive stage for rap's biggest names, giving early exposure to future superstars, including Travis Scott, Juice WRLD, and Ice Spice, and inspiring some of the most memorable moments in the genre's recent history. Under the leadership of founders Matt Zingler and Tariq Cherif, Rolling Loud has become a leading brand in the world of streetwear, with merchandise included in prominent retail establishments across the country. Now, Rolling Loud is hip-hop’s biggest stage, with star-studded festivals across the globe and more milestones to come.

Rolling Loud Miami is the crown jewel celebration of “10 years of Rolling Loud.” The party started in March with Rolling Loud California, returning to Hollywood Park, on the grounds adjacent to SoFi Stadium, in Inglewood, CA. Headlined by Nicki Minaj, Post Malone, and Future x Metro Boomin, the California festival was just the beginning of the party, which continued at Rolling Loud Europe, headlined by Travis Scott, Nicki Minaj, and Playboi Carti, in July.

The next stop on Rolling Loud's world tour is Rolling Loud Thailand, featuring artists like headliners Playboi Carti, A$AP Rocky, Wiz Khalifa, Tyla, and more. Rolling Loud's second-ever event in Asia returns to Pattaya, Thailand November 22nd-24th. Stay tuned for more additions to the Thailand lineup.

Stay tuned for many more announcements about Rolling Loud Miami and other upcoming events, and be sure to follow Rolling Loud on Instagram for in-the-moment updates.

About Rolling Loud:

Founded by 7-time Billboard Hip-Hop Power Players Matt Zingler & Tariq Cherif as a one-day Miami show, Rolling Loud has grown into a global festival phenomenon, putting on shows on both coasts and in multiple countries across four continents. Rolling Loud is the World’s Largest Hip-Hop Festival, known for its expert curation of wide-ranging lineups that reflect the state of the genre. In 2023, Rolling Loud expanded its empire even further, hosting a series of star-studded festivals in all corners of the globe, including debuts in Thailand and Germany, plus its flagship affair, Rolling Loud Miami, which offered its most diverse and wide-ranging lineup to date. Throughout 2024, Rolling Loud commemorates "10 Years Of Rolling Loud," celebrating the festival's rise from humble origins to world dominance. Since the first Miami festival, Rolling Loud has evolved into a major force in hip-hop, providing a massive stage for rap's biggest names, giving early exposure to future superstars, including Travis Scott, Juice WRLD, and Ice Spice, and inspiring some of the most memorable moments in the genre's recent history. Now, Rolling Loud is hip-hop’s biggest stage, with star-studded festivals on four continents and more milestones to come. Stay tuned for announcements about Rolling Loud's 2024 World Tour.
