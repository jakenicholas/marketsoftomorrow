W Pompano Beach Hotel & Residences, the cornerstone of what's to come

Once an overlooked waterfront village, Pompano Beach is cementing itself as a premier South Florida destination, driven by a wave of...

By Jake Nicholas April 3, 2025

Once an overlooked waterfront village, Pompano Beach is cementing itself as a premier South Florida destination, driven by a wave of boutique residential developments, a slew of vibrant new dining concepts, and exciting cultural additions. 

Originally catalyzed by multi-billion dollar investments across its downtown and iconic Fishing Village and Pier, Pompano Beach’s growth is further accelerated by the arrival of revered hospitality brands like The Ritz-Carlton and Waldorf Astoria. 

“Pompano Beach is firmly in the spotlight,” said Nick Perez, President of Related Group’s condominium group. “As the city’s evolution accelerates with new investment and a revitalized waterfront, its draw as a premier hub for contemporary coastal living just minutes from Miami and Fort Lauderdale will only get stronger.”

A shining example of Pompano Beach’s upward trajectory, the newly unveiled W Pompano Beach Hotel & Residences is poised to become the city’s most desirable address, bringing both luxury and liveliness to the heart of the waterfront. Developed by Related Group, in collaboration with BH Group and hospitality leader Marriott International, this project is setting the stage for the destination’s next chapter.

“W Pompano Beach is the cornerstone of what we’re creating in Pompano Beach. The city has evolved from a quiet beachfront community into a true destination, and this project—our fourth in the area—marks a new chapter in that transformation,” adds Perez. “With W Hotels, we’re introducing true five-star hotel accommodations—something discerning clientele haven’t experienced here before—along with luxury residences, world-class design, entertainment, and service that sets a new standard for the market.”

This 24-story luxury tower seamlessly blends W Hotels’ signature resort-style experience with contemporary residential living. Designed by Nichols Architects in collaboration with KORA, with award-winning interiors by Meyer Davis, the development features an exclusive collection of condominium residences and fully furnished condo-hotel suites. Three penthouses, each with a private rooftop terrace, elevate the offering. Expansive floor-to-ceiling windows frame sweeping views of the Atlantic Ocean and Intracoastal Waterway, enhancing the sophisticated design and coastal allure of this landmark property.

The Residences are meticulously crafted to provide an unparalleled living experience, combining functionality with refined elegance. Each home features expansive balconies, well-appointed dens, powder rooms, walk-in pantries and laundry areas and spa-inspired primary bathrooms equipped with spacious walk-in showers, soaking tubs and sophisticated Kohler fixtures. Residents also benefit from a suite of exclusive services, including a 24-hour attended lobby, valet parking and private elevator access to each residence, ensuring privacy and convenience. 

Fully managed by Marriott International, the amenities at W Pompano Beach Hotel & Residences embody what has made the W brand iconic—transforming the concept of luxury living into a dynamic, community-focused lifestyle with bold design, vibrant energy, and a commitment to delivering unique, unforgettable experiences.

The open-air, 60,000 square foot WET® Pool Deck will be a beach club-inspired centerpiece of the property, featuring private cabanas, a full-service bar and grill, pickleball and padel courts, and a jacuzzi. Indoors, the brand’s signature Living Room will serve as an expansive gathering space and social hub, enriched by curated programming including wine and food tastings, live music, art exhibitions, cultural events, and more. A yet-to-be-announced award-winning restaurant concept will further elevate the property’s dining offerings, serving bold, intentional cuisine from breakfast through dinner. The state-of-the-art FIT® fitness center and Yoga Studio will be equipped with the latest cardio and weight training equipment and feature a full schedule of classes alongside a dedicated Yoga and Spin studio. The property’s AWAY® Spa will offer a tranquil retreat, boasting custom wellness treatments, a cold plunge, a jacuzzi, a sauna, steam rooms, and hair-and-nail services. 

Initial pricing for The Residences starts at $3.1 million, with The Suites starting at $800,000. Fortune International is the exclusive sales and marketing agent for W Pompano Beach. To schedule a tour, please visit WPompanoBeach.com or call 954.323.5047.
