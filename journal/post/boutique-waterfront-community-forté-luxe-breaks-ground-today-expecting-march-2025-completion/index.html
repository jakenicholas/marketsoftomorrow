Boutique waterfront community Forté Luxe breaks ground today, expecting March 2025 completion

By Kaitlyn Nicholas June 29, 2023

Forté Development has officially broke ground yesterday with the development of Forté Luxe, an exclusive boutique waterfront community in Jupiter, Florida.

The development will consist of 15 multi-level residences with breathtaking views of the Intracoastal Waterway. The prices for these luxury homes range from $4.35 million to over $8 million, and the project is expected to be completed by March 2025. Forté Luxe aims to provide residents with a sophisticated coastal lifestyle in a highly sought-after area known for its natural beauty and year-round favorable weather. The development will feature a four-story architectural masterpiece designed by Alfonso Jurado, along with interior designs by The Decorators Unlimited. The residences will offer spacious living spaces, private garages with grand entries, waterfront terraces and balconies, designer kitchens and bathrooms, and premium finishes throughout. The community will also include private boat docks, a resort-style swimming pool, and various other amenities. The groundbreaking ceremony yesterday was attended by distinguished guests and marks a significant milestone in the creation of this waterfront community. Interested buyers can visit the Forté Luxe Sales Center to learn more and secure their place in this exclusive community.
