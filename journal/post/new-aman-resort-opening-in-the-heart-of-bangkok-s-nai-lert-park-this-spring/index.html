New Aman resort opening in the heart of Bangkok's Nai Lert Park this spring

Aman Nai Lert Bangkok is set to open this April within the seven-acre Nai Lert Park , offering a tranquil retreat in the heart of...

By Kaitlyn Nicholas February 10, 2025

Aman Nai Lert Bangkok is set to open this April within the seven-acre Nai Lert Park, offering a tranquil retreat in the heart of Thailand’s capital. This project marks Aman’s second property in Thailand, building on the legacy of Amanpuri, the brand’s flagship resort located on the shores of Phuket.

Designed by Jean-Michel Gathy of Denniston, the development integrates contemporary design with traditional Thai elements, drawing inspiration from the Nai Lert Park Heritage Home.

The hotel’s 52 suites, starting at 1,012 square feet, feature floor-to-ceiling windows that provide views of the park and city skyline. The Aman Suite, occupying an entire floor, spans 7,674 square feet and includes three bedrooms, a cinema and entertainment room, and a private spa.

Aman Nai Lert Bangkok’s 16,145-square-foot Aman Spa & Wellness centre spans three floors, offering facilities such as cryotherapy, IV therapy, steam rooms, saunas, hydrotherapy areas, and movement studios for yoga and Pilates. The spa includes a Medical Wellness space operated by Hertitude Clinic, providing specialized treatments.

Dining options at the property include Arva, an Italian restaurant, and 1872, a lounge and bar on the ninth floor. The Pool bar serves drinks by the infinity pool, while the 19th floor features Sesui, an omakase dining experience, and Hiori, a teppanyaki counter. The Aman Lounge and Cigar Bar provide additional spaces for socializing.

Located in one of Bangkok’s prestigious neighborhoods, Aman Nai Lert Bangkok offers an exclusive gateway near the city’s cultural landmarks.
