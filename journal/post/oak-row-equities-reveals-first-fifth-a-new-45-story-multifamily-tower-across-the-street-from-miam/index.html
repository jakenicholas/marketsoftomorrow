Oak Row Equities reveals First & Fifth, a new 45-story multifamily tower across the street from MiamiCentral, designed by ODP Architecture & Design

Oak Row Equities has secured a strategic location (49 Northwest 5th Street) in Downtown Miami for their upcoming project, "First &...

By Jake Nicholas June 18, 2024

Oak Row Equities has secured a strategic location (49 Northwest 5th Street) in Downtown Miami for their upcoming project, "First & Fifth." This 700,000-square-foot development will be a 45-story multifamily tower boasting 500 luxurious units.

Image: First & Fifth. Designed by ODP Architecture & Design

Drawing inspiration from Miami's early 20th-century Venetian architecture, the design incorporates prominent arches reminiscent of the Doge Palace in Venice. ODP Architecture & Design is the mastermind behind the project, ensuring a harmonious blend of historical influence with modern features and amenities.

Image: First & Fifth. Designed by ODP Architecture & Design

First & Fifth boasts an unmatched location directly across from MiamiCentral Station, the central hub for South Florida's major train systems: Brightline, Metrorail, Tri-Rail, and Metromover. This prime spot offers residents effortless access to various destinations throughout Miami and the surrounding region.

Image: First & Fifth. Designed by ODP Architecture & Design

This development aligns perfectly with Downtown Miami's growth trajectory. Residents will enjoy close proximity to MiamiCentral's new office buildings, Miami Dade College's Wolfson Campus, the federal courthouse, Miami Worldcenter's vibrant retail and dining scene, and the Miami Heat's arena.
