Life Time Miami at The Falls debuts 120,000 square-foot athletic and country club

In an unprecedented move for Miami's shopping scene, Life Time, the leading healthy lifestyle brand in the nation, unveiled its...

By Jake Nicholas August 14, 2023

In an unprecedented move for Miami's shopping scene, Life Time, the leading healthy lifestyle brand in the nation, unveiled its innovative athletic country club experience at The Falls. This milestone marks Life Time's second establishment in greater Miami and its fourth in South Florida, as part of the brand's expansive nationwide growth.

The newly unveiled Life Time Miami at The Falls boasts a sprawling 120,000-square-foot complex, comprising a three-story building and an outdoor beach club, setting a new benchmark for health and wellness offerings in the area. Situated within Simon's upscale 55-acre shopping, dining, and entertainment haven, this three-story athletic club and its interconnected beach club, complete with saltwater pools, waterslides, cabanas, and a café and bar, introduces a fresh and captivating anchor to The Falls, enhancing foot traffic and customer experiences.

"Life Time at The Falls introduces an entirely novel concept to Miami, and stands as our sixth collaboration with Simon, aligned with our asset-light strategy," commented Parham Javaheri, Chief Property Development Officer at Life Time. "We're excited for the residents of Miami to immerse themselves in the unparalleled programs and offerings that Life Time brings to The Falls, contributing to Simon's commitment of crafting novel experiences for their patrons."

Life Time Miami at The Falls offers a comprehensive suite of health-focused amenities for individuals spanning 90 days to 90 years old. These include seven specialized studios offering over 130 weekly sessions of barre, cycling, group fitness, Pilates, and yoga. The expansive fitness floor houses top-tier cardio and resistance training equipment, while dedicated small-group training areas and three indoor pickleball courts cater to varied fitness preferences. Further enriching the experience are indoor and outdoor lap, leisure, and whirlpools, accompanied by rejuvenation suites featuring steam rooms, saunas, and cold plunge pools, all set within opulent dressing rooms. Members can also relish the convenience of a fast-casual Life Cafe restaurant and lounge, a full-fledged LifeSpa and salon, and an exclusive Kids Academy. Additionally, a dedicated Life Time Work Club Lounge space is available, catering to those seeking a productive environment.

This inauguration at The Falls marks the fourth establishment for Life Time in South Florida, alongside Life Time Palm Beach Gardens (2022), Life Time Coral Gables (2020), and Life Time Boca Raton (2006). Notably, Life Time's footprint has been steadily expanding in south Florida, where it holds a prominent role as the long-standing organizer and host of the renowned Miami Marathon, set to return for its 22nd iteration on January 28, 2024. Among Life Time's wide-reaching presence are other collaborations with Simon properties, including Houston Galleria in Houston, Texas, Northshore Mall in Peabody, Mass., Southdale Center in Edina, Minn., The Shops at Riverside in Hackensack, New Jersey, and Phipps Plaza in Atlanta, Georgia, in addition to its presence in 12 other shopping centers across the nation.
