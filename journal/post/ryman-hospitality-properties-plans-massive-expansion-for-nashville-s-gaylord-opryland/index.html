Ryman Hospitality Properties plans massive expansion for Nashville's Gaylord Opryland

In 2027, the iconic Gaylord Opryland Resort & Convention Center will mark its golden 50th anniversary with a spectacular $224 million...

By Jake Nicholas February 5, 2025

In 2027, the iconic Gaylord Opryland Resort & Convention Center will mark its golden 50th anniversary with a spectacular $224 million renovation and expansion. This ambitious, multiphase project is more than just an upgrade—it's a bold vision designed to solidify Opryland's status as the crown jewel of Ryman Hospitality Properties Inc., drawing in premier corporate clientele while embracing Nashville's dynamic growth as a top-tier business and leisure destination.

The transformation will unfold in three thoughtfully planned phases, each crafted to elevate both the aesthetic appeal and functional versatility of this beloved landmark.

Phase 1, set to reach completion by mid-year, focuses on breathing new life into the distinguished Governor's and Presidential ballrooms. Spanning an impressive 133,000 square feet, these revitalized spaces will offer state-of-the-art amenities and refined design touches, perfect for hosting high-profile events. Adjacent to these ballrooms, the former Magnolia Pool Courtyard will be reimagined into the vibrant $40 million Magnolia District.

This dynamic new hub will feature a sprawling 6,200-square-foot event lawn, an elegant 3,000-square-foot pavilion, and a striking multistory sports bar with seating for 550 guests, complete with a sophisticated tasting room. Designed for versatility, these venues will cater to private events of all kinds, offering live entertainment, interactive games, and an eclectic range of culinary experiences.

Phase 2 is already underway, with significant progress being made toward its anticipated year-end completion. This phase will continue the momentum, enhancing the resort's offerings with innovative spaces and modern amenities tailored to meet the evolving demands of today’s event planners and guests.

The final phase will introduce a striking structure along Briley Parkway, adding an additional 108,000 square feet of premium, carpeted meeting spaces. These new spaces are meticulously designed to support corporate gatherings, with an emphasis on flexible breakout areas that foster collaboration.

Once completed in 2027, Opryland will feature an impressive total of 765,000 square feet of state-of-the-art meeting facilities, positioning it as one of the most expansive and versatile convention destinations in the country.

This visionary project is not just about expanding square footage; it's about reimagining what a premier convention center can be. By blending modern sophistication with the charm of Southern hospitality, the Gaylord Opryland Resort & Convention Center is poised to usher in a new era, reflecting Nashville’s vibrant spirit and continued rise as a global destination for both business and leisure.
