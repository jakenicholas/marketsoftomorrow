JO/GA Homes launch sales for La Corte, a luxurious self-sufficient LEED Platinum certified home complete with photovoltaic solar setup and Tesla batteries

The innovative minds behind JO/GA Homes, Gaia Sonzogni and Jorge Campodonico, revealed their latest masterpiece in Miami's Nautilus area,...

By Jake Nicholas March 8, 2024

The innovative minds behind JO/GA Homes, Gaia Sonzogni and Jorge Campodonico, revealed their latest masterpiece in Miami's Nautilus area, "La Corte." This luxurious 4,162-square-foot home, now on the market for $7.499 million through Caervera Real Estate, embodies their pioneering approach to combining sophistication with eco-friendly living in Miami's dynamic landscape.

Nestled at 4354 Michigan Ave, Miami Beach, "La Corte," or "the courtyard" in Italian, is designed to be more than just a place to live. It's a carefully crafted environment where sustainability and style meet to elevate the living experience. The home's 2,000 square feet of outdoor space centers around a lush courtyard, creating a tranquil oasis in the city. Its design prioritizes fluid movement and privacy, supported by passive architectural principles.

La Corte's green initiatives are notable, including a stormwater collection system and an energy-efficient structure that promises over 65% in energy savings. Coupled with a photovoltaic solar setup and Tesla batteries, the home is fully self-sufficient, aiming for LEED Platinum certification as a testament to its environmental stewardship.

The interior design of La Corte reflects a blend of contemporary and organic aesthetics, with a focus on natural finishes and eco-friendly materials that harmonize with Miami's coastal landscape. The use of feng shui principles further enhances the space, fostering a sense of balance and well-being throughout the home.

The launch of La Corte signifies the beginning of JO/GA Homes' journey in redefining Miami's housing market through a lens of sustainability and luxury. With Sonzogni's design expertise and Campodonico's real estate acumen, JO/GA Homes is set to transform the concept of luxury living in Miami, making La Corte a beacon of their dedication to innovative, sustainable, and luxurious homes.

To get in touch with JO/GA Homes, please contact 786-777-8716 or info@jogahomes.com
