PMG and LNDMRK launch first-of-its-kind condo-office-Airbnb hybrid in Wynwood

A bold new residential concept is taking shape in Miami’s Wynwood Arts District. National developer PMG, in collaboration with LNDMRK...

By Jake Nicholas April 21, 2025

A bold new residential concept is taking shape in Miami’s Wynwood Arts District. National developer PMG, in collaboration with LNDMRK Development, has unveiled Twenty Sixth & 2nd Wynwood Residences—an eight-story, 233-unit luxury condominium project that introduces a first-of-its-kind offering: deeded office suites paired with fully Airbnb-permitted residences.

Set to launch sales on April 24, this groundbreaking development allows owners to host both their home and office 365 days a year on Airbnb, reflecting a growing demand for flexible living and working environments in one of Miami’s fastest-evolving neighborhoods.

As Miami solidifies its status as a year-round destination for both leisure and business, Wynwood has emerged as a magnet for innovation and investment. Recent commercial commitments from companies like Amazon and Gemini have elevated the area’s reputation as a hub for Class-A office space.

Twenty Sixth & 2nd responds directly to this shift, offering an integrated solution for residents seeking both high-end accommodations and functional office space within a vibrant urban core.

Each studio and one-bedroom unit at the property includes access to a dedicated private office suite—an offering that sets the project apart in the city’s competitive condo market.

The development will also feature more than 32,000 square feet of wellness-focused amenities, as well as 26,000 square feet of ground-floor retail with roughly 475 feet of linear frontage, poised to attract a dynamic mix of retailers and restaurants.

PMG Residential is leading the sales effort for Twenty Sixth & 2nd Wynwood Residences, positioning the project at the intersection of hospitality, flexibility, and modern urban living.
