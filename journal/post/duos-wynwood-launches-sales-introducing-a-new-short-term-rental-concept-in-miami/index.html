DUOS Wynwood launches sales, introducing a new short-term rental concept in Miami

DUOS Wynwood has officially launched condo sales in the heart of Miami's Wynwood Arts District, introducing a 49-unit, eight-story condominium project purpose-built for short-term rental flexibility.

By Jake Nicholas May 19, 2026

DUOS Wynwood has officially launched condo sales in the heart of Miami's Wynwood Arts District, introducing a 49-unit, eight-story condominium project purpose-built for short-term rental flexibility in one of South Florida's fastest-growing neighborhoods.

Developed by Wave Group Development, Vitrium Capital, and Zambala Luxury Apartments, DUOS Wynwood marks a clear shift away from the traditional condo and hotel-branded residence models that have dominated Miami's recent development cycle. Rather than adapting a typical condo product to accommodate flexible stays, DUOS was purpose-designed from the ground up to give owners full ownership of their unit alongside the ability to rent it out for stays ranging from a single night up to 30 days. It's a meaningful distinction in a market where most short-term rental opportunities are workarounds rather than the original intent.

Located at 335 Northwest 28th Street in the center of the Wynwood Arts District, the development introduces studio to two-bedroom condo suites ranging from the $500,000s to over $1 million. Construction is expected to break ground in late 2026, with completion anticipated in early 2028.

What sets DUOS apart isn't just the model—it's the scale. With just 49 units, the project offers a more curated, boutique alternative to the larger-scale Miami developments that often exceed 200 units. Studio, one-, and two-bedroom layouts range from 400 to 900 square feet, designed to flex between short visits and longer stays in a way Airbnbs and traditional hotels can't replicate.

Design plays a defining role in the project's identity. Architecture comes from acclaimed firm MKDA, with interiors by award-winning international hospitality design firm AvroKo—the team behind some of the most distinctive hospitality concepts in the world. Together, they've shaped a non-branded, design-driven identity rooted in eclectic modernism, where minimalist architectural forms are layered with bold colors, textures, and storytelling. Blending architecture, branding, and atmosphere into a single cohesive experience, AvroKo's involvement signals that DUOS is being positioned as a design-forward destination, not just another investment vehicle.

Inside, programming reflects how today's global buyers actually live and travel. Inspired spaces include a landscaped paseo arrival, an art-filled lobby lounge, a coffee bar, and a fitness center, alongside dedicated co-working spaces with private, acoustically insulated video-conferencing rooms and a tech-enabled meeting room. Site-specific murals and integrated lifestyle and security technology further enhance the experience, while 3,000 square feet of ground-floor retail space envisioned for food and beverage contributes to the building's active urban presence.

The timing reflects a deeper shift happening in Wynwood itself. Once known primarily as Miami's creative and gallery district, the neighborhood has rapidly evolved into one of the city's most active corporate corridors, with global names like Amazon, Spotify, Gemini, Sony Music, and PwC establishing a presence in recent years. That influx is driving a new kind of demand—travelers, executives, and remote workers looking for hospitality-driven stays that feel more residential than transactional.

"DUOS Wynwood was planned with a clear view of the shift in how people travel, work, and vacation," said Alessandro Ferretti, Principal at Wave Group Development. "There is a growing presence of global corporations moving into Wynwood like Amazon, Spotify, Gemini, Sony Music and PwC, to name a few, and we're offering a compelling opportunity for buyers who want the benefits of ownership plus the flexibility of short-term rentals in one of Miami's most in-demand neighborhoods."

The team behind the project carries deep development and hospitality experience across Miami, Italy, and South America. Wave Group Development brings over 30 years of experience developing boutique hospitality and luxury residential properties in Miami; Vitrium Capital has developed and managed residential and mixed-use projects throughout the Americas; and Zambala Luxury Apartments contributes operational and design expertise in hospitality-driven apartment living, with international recognition including selection by Olympic delegations and inclusion in the Louis Vuitton Milan City Guide.

Sales and marketing are being led exclusively by Brown Harris Stevens Development Marketing and Brown Harris Stevens Miami, with Morgan Blittner leading sales. An in-person sales center is located at Brown Harris Stevens Miami's Continuum office in Miami Beach's South of Fifth neighborhood at 40 South Pointe Drive, Suite 110.

"For buyers seeking exposure to Miami's hospitality-driven real estate market, DUOS offers an exciting opportunity to jump in," said Morgan Blittner of Brown Harris Stevens Miami. "Buyers are increasingly looking for turnkey assets in walkable, mixed-use districts like Wynwood. DUOS delivers with style and at a highly competitive price point."

With sales now officially underway, with starting prices below $500K, and a 2028 delivery on the horizon, DUOS Wynwood is positioning itself as one of the more distinctive new condo launches in Miami's residential pipeline.
