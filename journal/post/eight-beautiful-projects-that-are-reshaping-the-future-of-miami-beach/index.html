Eight beautiful projects that are reshaping the future of Miami Beach

Miami Beach is undergoing a dazzling transformation, with a wave of luxurious new developments redefining its skyline and lifestyle...

By Jake Nicholas January 8, 2025

Miami Beach is undergoing a dazzling transformation, with a wave of luxurious new developments redefining its skyline and lifestyle offerings. From world-class residences by iconic brands like Ritz-Carlton and Aman to groundbreaking hotel and condo hybrids like The Shore Club and The Raleigh, each project promises an elevated blend of architectural excellence, bespoke amenities, and unparalleled oceanfront living. These upcoming developments are not just reshaping the landscape—they’re setting new standards for luxury and sophistication in one of the world’s most vibrant coastal cities.

Ritz-Carlton Residences, South Beach

Set to redefine luxury living, the Ritz-Carlton Residences, South Beach, will bring an exclusive collection of 30 custom-designed homes to one of Miami Beach's last prime beachfront locations. This 15-story condominium, a collaboration between Lionstone Development, the Ben-Josef Group, and Flag Luxury Group, will include two opulent duplex penthouses priced up to $125 million. Designed by Kobi Karp with interiors by Alessandro Munge, the residences will feature private elevators, wraparound terraces, and breathtaking views. Residents will enjoy 50,000 square feet of bespoke amenities, including dining by Michelin-starred Chef José Andrés, a spa, and an oceanfront beach club. Completion is expected by April 2027, with sales already underway at the Ritz-Carlton South Beach sales gallery.

The Raleigh Rosewood Residences

The Raleigh Rosewood Residences will merge the timeless charm of the historic Raleigh Hotel with a new 17-story tower featuring 40 waterfront homes. Set for completion in 2026, this development, led by SHVO and designed by Peter Marino, will include a restored 60-suite hotel, a private members' club, and residences ranging from two to seven bedrooms, priced from $10 million. The penthouses, spanning up to 13,218 square feet, will boast expansive terraces with ocean views. Amenities include sunrise and sunset pools, a private spa, and three Michelin-starred restaurants. This project aims to honor the Raleigh’s Art Deco legacy while offering a contemporary luxury lifestyle.

Aman Miami Beach

Nestled in Miami Beach's vibrant Faena District, Aman Miami Beach promises an oasis of tranquility with just 22 exclusive residences. Designed by Kengo Kuma, the homes blend Art Deco inspiration with Japanese minimalism, seamlessly integrating indoor and outdoor spaces. Residents will enjoy access to a private pool, Aman Spa, and dining venues, along with priority access to the Aman Miami Beach hotel in the historic Versailles building. Opening in 2026, this development offers a serene retreat while embracing Miami Beach’s iconic charm.

Ella Miami Beach

Paying homage to jazz legend Ella Fitzgerald, Ella Miami Beach introduces 95 boutique residences located just two blocks from the Atlantic Ocean in North Beach. With unique layouts ranging from studios to two-bedroom homes, this community celebrates Miami’s glamorous past while offering modern comforts. Residents will enjoy over 22,000 square feet of curated amenities, including a rooftop infinity pool, padel court, spa, yoga lawn, and resident lounge, all with sweeping views of the skyline and ocean. Ella Miami Beach embodies the vibrant spirit of the area while delivering a refined living experience.

The Perigon Miami Beach

The Perigon is an architectural masterpiece by OMA, offering 73 ultra-luxury residences on Miami Beach's pristine coastline. Each home features private elevator entry, floor-to-ceiling windows, and expansive terraces with Atlantic views. Designed with interiors by Tara Bernerd and lush landscaping by Gustafson Porter + Bowman, the residences showcase Italian kitchens, bespoke finishes, and spacious living areas. Amenities include a Michelin-starred restaurant, speakeasy, spa, fitness center, and private beach access. Developed by Mast Capital and Starwood Capital Group, The Perigon sets a new standard for sophisticated oceanfront living.

Grand Hyatt Convention Center Hotel

The long-anticipated Grand Hyatt Miami Beach will anchor the Miami Beach Convention Center District, offering 800 luxury guest rooms across 12 floors. Developed by Terra Group and Turnberry Associates, the hotel will feature resort-style amenities, including a pool deck with panoramic views, event spaces, and retail outlets. An elevated skybridge will connect the hotel to the Convention Center, providing seamless access for attendees. This transformative project, approved with $92.5 million in agency debt, will solidify Miami Beach’s reputation as a premier destination for conventions and luxury accommodations.

The Alton Miami Beach

The Alton will redefine Miami Beach’s office market with its cutting-edge design by Foster + Partners. This Class A commercial space will feature 170,000 square feet of office space, 17,000 square feet of ground-floor retail, and five luxury residences. The building will emphasize outdoor spaces, including grand terraces with panoramic views of Miami Beach. SHVO’s collaboration with Kobi Karp and Foster + Partners ensures a landmark project that combines sustainability, innovation, and elevated standards for office spaces. Positioned at the gateway to Lincoln Road, The Alton promises to be a standout addition to Miami Beach’s skyline.

The Shore Club Resort & Residences, Auberge Resorts Collection

The Shore Club Resort & Residences will seamlessly blend historic Art Deco elegance with contemporary luxury. The project will restore the iconic Shore Club Hotel, adding a modern 18-story tower with 49 exclusive condos. Residences, starting at $6 million, will feature spacious layouts, expansive terraces, and high-end finishes. The development will also include a fitness center, beach club, spa, and signature Auberge restaurant. A potential record-breaking $120 million penthouse sale highlights the project's prestige. Scheduled for completion in 2027, this collaboration between Witkoff, Monroe Capital, and Auberge Resorts Collection will redefine Miami Beach luxury living.
