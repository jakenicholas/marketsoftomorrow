Oil Nut Bay unveils new luxury villas and first overwater spa in the British Virgin Islands

Nestled in Virgin Gorda’s North Sound, Oil Nut Bay is a private island resort offering an exclusive retreat across 400 pristine acres....

By Jake Nicholas February 6, 2025

Nestled in Virgin Gorda’s North Sound, Oil Nut Bay is a private island resort offering an exclusive retreat across 400 pristine acres. Known for its world-class villas, vibrant marina, and laid-back boating lifestyle, the resort is set to debut the first overwater spa in the British Virgin Islands this spring.

In addition, several new luxury villas are now available for rent, including Wings—a sprawling estate featuring six free-standing bedroom suites, each with a private plunge pool, outdoor shower, and kitchenette.

Opening in spring 2025, Sundara Spa will offer an immersive wellness experience inspired by the sea and surrounding landscape. Its name, derived from the Sanskrit word for beauty, reflects the spa’s tranquil setting over the crystal-clear waters of the BVI. Designed with nearly 360-degree glass walls and underwater windows, the spa will feature:

  • A hospitality bar and retail boutique

  • A hair and nail salon

  • Multiple treatment rooms

  • A wellness studio with retractable glass walls for open-air or climate-controlled sessions, including yoga, Pilates, sound baths, and guided meditations

Wings Villa (Starting at $12,000/night)

The most expansive estate available for nightly stays, Wings offers six private bedroom suite “pods,” each featuring a plunge pool, outdoor shower, ensuite bathroom, and sitting area. A seventh pod, The Nest, serves as the estate’s social hub, complete with floor-to-ceiling windows, a gourmet kitchen, a dining area, and an office.

Quintessa Villa (Starting at $5,450/night)

This 4,154-square-foot, multi-level retreat features five bedrooms, including a private honeymoon suite, each with its own terrace, ensuite bathroom, and outdoor shower. Designed for seamless indoor-outdoor living, the villa boasts panoramic ocean views and lush tropical surroundings.

Casino Royale (Available March 2025, Starting at $7,250/night)

A waterfront estate designed for privacy and comfort, Casino Royale features six suites, each with an ensuite bathroom and ample lounge space, plus a multi-purpose bunk/game room—perfect for families. The villa’s highlights include a sprawling great room, fully equipped gourmet kitchen, infinity pool, fire pit, and outdoor dining areas.

Guests at Oil Nut Bay enjoy access to a reimagined beach club with three pools and a swim-up bar, a wellness center with pickleball and tennis courts, the Nut House Kids Club, and the lively Marina Village, home to Nova, the resort’s overwater restaurant and bar. Unique island experiences include turtle tagging, kite surfing, seabobbing, and rum tastings curated from the resort’s Rum Bible.

New direct flights from Miami to Tortola on American Airlines provide seamless access, with nightly rates ranging from $650 to $17,950 and homesites starting at $1.95M. With spring break travel on the horizon, Oil Nut Bay presents a stunning escape for those seeking luxury, adventure, and relaxation in the heart of the Caribbean.
