Ora by Casa Tua, a 76-story mixed-use tower offering short-term residences is coming to Miami's bustling Brickell financial district

By Jake Nicholas December 31, 2023

In March, Fortune International Group announced the launch of ORA by Casa Tua, a new and exciting short-term rental residence in Miami's Brickell neighborhood. Located at 1210 Brickell, this 76-story mixed-use development features 540 residences, poised to become some of Miami's most sought-after short-term rental options.

ORA by Casa Tua is more than just a residence; it represents a new way of living in the vibrant heart of Brickell. The collaboration between Casa Tua and Fortune International Group brings together ORA’s impeccably curated residences with world-class amenities and soulful cuisine to create a unique residential experience in Miami’s thriving market.

This development includes approximately 25,000 square feet of space dedicated to Casa Tua’s four new restaurant concepts: Terra, Uva, Fuoco, and Vento. These venues range from a 24-hour market and bakery to a dream enoteca with hundreds of sommelier-selected labels and exquisite Mediterranean cuisine, an intimate dining experience celebrating simple cooking, and a rooftop bar and lounge complemented by a pool overlooking Brickell, Biscayne Bay, and Coconut Grove.

In addition to the culinary delights, ORA features creative workspaces with private office spaces, conference rooms, content creation rooms, a bar lounge, and outdoor seating. Residents can also enjoy resort-style offerings such as a second pool on the 12th floor, a show kitchen, private event space, lush green landscaping, an entertainment lounge, a two-floor fitness and wellness center, and a three-story open sky garden, which will be a unique feature in Miami.

The design of ORA, by the globally recognized award-winning architecture firm Arquitectonica, reflects the evolving nature of society, connecting multiple outdoor levels to nature and the environment. The interiors, both for the amenities and residential spaces, are designed by m2atelier, known for their bespoke approach.

The residences, managed by Fortune International Group, range from 500-square-foot studio homes to 2,400-square-foot four-bedroom fully furnished residences, all equipped with panoramic views, ten-foot ceilings with floor-to-ceiling windows, Italian-designed kitchens and baths, smart home technology, high-speed internet & Wi-Fi, spacious walk-in closets, and washers and dryers. The starting price for these residences is $800,000.

Fortune International Group, under the leadership of visionary founder Edgardo Defortuna, has a history of successful developments, including St. Regis Residences, Sunny Isles Beach; The Ritz-Carlton Residences, Pompano Beach; and Nexo Residences in North Miami Beach.

Casa Tua, founded by Miky and Leticia Grendene, has expanded its footprint since opening its flagship Miami Beach location in 2001, pioneering the members club concept in Miami and combining art, entertainment, and fine dining with a signature Italian style.
