Bespoke unveils first turnkey estate on Fisher Island

Bespoke Real Estate has just unveiled one of Fisher Island’s most significant residential milestones: Villa 6 at 1006 Links Drive — the first fully turnkey home completed within The Links Estates.

By Jake Nicholas January 27, 2026

Bespoke Real Estate has just unveiled one of Fisher Island’s most significant residential milestones: Villa 6 at 1006 Links Drive — the first fully turnkey home completed within The Links Estates, a private enclave of just 12 single-family estates on America’s most exclusive private island.

Sited on 0.43 acres± directly abutting Fisher Island’s famed golf course, this residence presents an extraordinary opportunity for buyers looking to step into the pinnacle of Miami-area real estate with zero construction risk and immediate enjoyment.

Villa 6 delivers more than 12,470± square feet of interior living space — alongside 4,510± square feet of exterior living areas — thoughtfully composed for both grand entertaining and relaxed daily living.

The home features:

  • Six generously scaled bedrooms, including a private primary suite retreat with expansive proportions.

  • Seven full and two half bathrooms, all exquisitely finished.

  • Architecturally engaging spaces that balance flow, light, and volume, from the dramatic foyer to the open living rooms and formal dining.

Designed for seamless indoor/outdoor living, Villa 6’s exterior spaces are equally impressive. Signature landscape architecture by Enzo Enea frames lush gardens, while a zero-edge heated gunite pool and spa offer a private resort experience. A rooftop terrace with a summer kitchen creates an elevated setting for sunset gatherings with fairway and water vistas.

The estate was brought to life by a team of elite creators: architectural vision by Portuondo Perotti Architects, interiors by Christina Leon of Ford Interiors, and landscape design by Enea Garden Design — together shaping an environment of refinement without compromise.

Accessible only by private boat, ferry, or helicopter, Fisher Island spans roughly 216 acres± and has long been one of the nation’s wealthiest ZIP codes, prized for privacy and prestige. The island’s amenities include the historic Vanderbilt Mansion clubhouse, championship golf, racquet club, luxury marina options, dining and social venues, private beach access, and 24/7 security — all serving a global community of residents.

Villa 6’s completion marks a notable moment on Fisher Island: the first move-in-ready estate of its kind in a limited collection of homes engineered for turnkey living. With luxury markets in South Florida continuing to favor exceptional, ready-to-occupy product, this property stands as one of the most compelling offerings in the region this year.
