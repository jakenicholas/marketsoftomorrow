BD Hotels and developers break ground on The Nora Hotel in the new $1 billion Nora District in West Palm Beach

BD Hotels , along with partners NDT Development , Place Projects , and Wheelock Street Capital , has officially broken ground on The Nora...

By Jake Nicholas November 14, 2024

BD Hotels, along with partners NDT Development, Place Projects, and Wheelock Street Capital, has officially broken ground on The Nora Hotel, setting the stage for a new era of sophisticated, boutique hospitality in West Palm Beach.

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Stantec

This milestone not only brings BD Hotels' high-profile design expertise to Florida for the first time but also signifies the Nora District’s ambitious plans to become a vibrant cultural and social hub in the heart of the city. As the Nora District continues to take shape, The Nora Hotel will serve as a cornerstone of the district, offering a thoughtfully curated experience that blends style, history, and the warmth of Florida’s hospitality scene.

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Gachot Studios

Richard Born of BD Hotels expressed the group’s vision for the new hotel: "The Nora Hotel will bring an elegant, high-design experience to West Palm Beach, appealing to today’s sophisticated travelers. We’re thrilled to collaborate with the talented Nora District team to make this unique destination a reality."

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Gachot Studios

BD Hotels is renowned for some of New York City’s most celebrated hotels, including The Greenwich, The Bowery, and The Mercer. The Nora Hotel also signifies BD Hotels' second collaboration with Wheelock Street Capital, following the successful relaunch of NYC’s Hotel Chelsea in 2023.

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Gachot Studios

With interior design by Gachot Studios and architecture by Stantec, The Nora Hotel draws inspiration from the Mediterranean and Spanish Colonial Revival styles of the 20th-century architect Addison Mizner, integrating features such as arched passageways, rich materials, and intricate tile work throughout. Guests will be welcomed by a traditional loggia and garden, creating an immersive, serene experience.

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Gachot Studios

The Nora Hotel will also be home to the celebrated Parisian-inspired brasserie Pastis, brought to life by James Beard Award-winning restaurateur Stephen Starr (STARR Restaurants). The ground floor will offer over 13,000 square feet of dining, with additional STARR-managed experiences, including a rooftop restaurant and bar, as well as in-room dining.

The Nora Hotel, Image Credit: Pastis

The Nora District, developed in collaboration with the City of West Palm Beach and designed by urban planning firm PlusUrbia, is set to redefine the city’s downtown scene. Phase I of the district will include 150,000 square feet of retail and office spaces. Anchored by the charm of renovated railway warehouses, Nora will feature a curated mix of local and national retailers and public art installations, creating a vibrant space for the community.

The Nora District, Image Credit: ASD Sky

Confirmed tenants in Nora’s first phase include Loco Taqueria & Oyster Bar, H&H Bagels, Van Leeuwen Ice Cream, The Garret Group, Juliana’s Pizza, Celis Juice Bar and Café, Del Mar Mediterranean Restaurant, Sana Skin Studio, Mint, The Spot Barbershop, [solidcore] and Sunday Motor Co. with more to be announced.

The Nora District, Image Credit: ASD Sky
