Terra and THE WELL announce their second wellness-focused residential project in Miami

Terra and THE WELL are bringing their second wellness-focused residential project to Miami with plans for THE WELL Coconut Grove , a $650...

By Jake Nicholas December 29, 2024

Terra and THE WELL are bringing their second wellness-focused residential project to Miami with plans for THE WELL Coconut Grove, a $650 million development located at 2855 Tigertail Avenue. Following the success of THE WELL Bay Harbor, this new project will integrate wellness-centered design into high-end residential living.

The residences will range from one to four bedrooms, spanning 960 to 4,200 square feet, with prices starting at $1.5 million.

Designed to prioritize holistic well-being, the development’s standout amenities include hyperbaric chambers for healing, crystal cave relaxation lounges, and spaces dedicated to physical and mental wellness.

As interest in wellness real estate continues to grow, the project has already seen strong demand. Within 48 hours of its announcement, 20% of units were reserved.

Coconut Grove, with its active lifestyle and connection to the outdoors, provides an ideal setting for this wellness-focused community. Miami, ranked among the healthiest cities in the U.S., aligns seamlessly with the development’s ethos of combining luxury living with health-conscious design.

THE WELL Coconut Grove aims to redefine modern living, blending high-end amenities with wellness-oriented features to meet the evolving desires of today’s homeowners.
