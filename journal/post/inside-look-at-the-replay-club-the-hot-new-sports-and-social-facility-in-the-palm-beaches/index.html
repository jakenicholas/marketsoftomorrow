Inside look at The Replay Club, the hot new sports and social facility in The Palm Beaches

By Jake Nicholas January 24, 2025

Nestled in the heart of Boynton Beach, Florida, The Replay Club is redefining the sports and social scene in Palm Beach County. This state-of-the-art facility spans over 70,000 square feet and is dedicated to providing an unparalleled experience for both pickleball and padel enthusiasts. 

World-Class Facilities

The Replay Club brings two massive and thoughtfully crafted buildings with unique experiences:

  • Padel Facility: A 50,000-square-foot space housing 10 indoor, air-conditioned padel courts. Players can enjoy regular play, clinics, tournaments, and a vibrant social atmosphere.

  • Pickleball Facility: A 20,000-square-foot area featuring 7 indoor, air-conditioned pickleball courts. This facility also includes an event space, outdoor area, pool table, locker rooms, and a children's play area, catering to players of all ages and skill levels. 

Beyond the Courts

The Replay Club is more than just a place to play; it's a community hub where members can connect, relax, and spend a day. Some of their amenities include:

  • Full-Service Gym: Beautifully equipped gym bringing new equipment, turf, and views of the padel facility.

  • Spa: Large sauna, steam room, and cold plunge.

  • Yoga Studio: An immersive sound studio with weekly programmed heated yoga classes and other exercise experiences.

  • Members Lounge: A modern mezzanine, Slim's makes unwinding with fellow members engaging with a bar, workstations, lounge couches, and views of the facility from another light.

Engaging Programs and Events

  • Monday League Night: Join an 8-week league for both pickleball and padel to enjoy competitive play and camaraderie.

  • Nacho Tuesdays: A popular event combining play with delicious nachos, perfect for meeting new people.

  • Open Play Wednesdays: Book ahead to participate in open play sessions, with occasional appearances by professional players.

  • Americano Thursdays: A format for intermediate padel players to compete with different partners in a fun, rotating setup.

  • Friday Night Lights: For 5.0 players, this night offers thrilling matches and a lively atmosphere in the lounge.

  • Après Saturdays: Spend the day at the club enjoying games, workouts, spa sessions, and relaxation in the sports lounge.

  • Sunday Tournaments: Weekly intermediate tournaments with football games played on the mezzanine.

Membership Options

The Replay Club offers several membership tiers to suit different needs:

  • Legend Membership: $450 per month, includes full access to spa, gym, members lounge, complimentary pickleball court fees, discounted padel court fees, fitness classes, guest passes, early booking access, and more.

  • Icon Membership: $250 per month, includes similar benefits with slight variations in class access, guest passes, and discounts.

  • Seasonal Icon Membership: $275 per month with a 6-month contractual agreement, offering flexibility for seasonal residents.

Additional household members can be added at discounted rates, and children under 13 can access the club under parental supervision or scheduled youth programming.

Join the Community

Just minutes from I-95 and a short drive from Boca Raton, Delray Beach, and West Palm Beach, find The Replay Club at 6600 & 7700 High Ridge Road, Boynton Beach, FL 33426. Whether you're aiming to compete, learn, or simply have fun, The Replay Club is truly proving to be one of The Palm Beaches' nicest clubs, bringing a dynamic environment to elevate your experience both on and off the court. For more information or to become a member, visit their website at www.thereplay.club
