Tampa Bay

NOVEL Beach Park offers residents access to Tampa's most sought-after activities

By Jake Nicholas December 17, 2024

Nestled along the serene shores of Old Tampa Bay, Beach Park offers a picturesque waterfront lifestyle. Lush lawns, majestic oak trees, and swaying palm trees create a tranquil ambiance throughout this beautiful neighborhood. Residents enjoy easy access to a variety of outdoor activities, including boating, fishing, and exploring the three nearby parks. Cypress Point Park, in particular, boasts a boardwalk, canoe dock, hiking trails, playgrounds, and more. With its resort-like atmosphere and convenient location just five miles west of downtown Tampa, Beach Park provides the perfect blend of natural beauty and urban convenience.

NOVEL Beach Park is ideally located near the upcoming redevelopment of Westshore Plaza, a massive project featuring 3.5 million square feet of shopping, dining, and entertainment. This exciting development will bring a variety of new retail options and experiences to the area. In addition to WestShore Plaza, residents of NOVEL Beach Park can also enjoy the convenience of nearby shopping centers like Town Square Center, all within a short walk or drive.

With over 25 upscale dining restaurants within easy reach, NOVEL Beach Park provides residents with a convenient and diverse culinary experience. Enjoy the flavors of the world without going far. From Élevage SoHo Kitchen & Bar to Ocean Prime, Olivia, and Seasons 52, there's something to satisfy every palate.

Enhance your knowledge and have fun at the five parks located near NOVEL Beach Park. Discover the wonders of the natural world at The Florida Aquarium and McKay Bay Nature Park, explore the rich history of Ybor City Museum State Park, and encounter fascinating animals at Lowry Park Zoo and Weedon Island Preserve.

Traveling to and from NOVEL Beach Park is easy with nearby airports and convenient transportation options. Tampa International Airport is just 4.1 miles away, while St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport is a 25-minute drive. For those arriving by car, the Skyconnect Economy Parking Station is located 2.5 miles away.

Residents of NOVEL Beach Park have easy access to a variety of educational opportunities. Hillsborough Community College, Dale Mabry Campus, is just a 10-minute drive away, while The University of Tampa and Hillsborough Community College, Ybor City Campus, are also within close proximity.

Enjoy the convenience of having top-rated hospitals nearby. NOVEL Beach Park is located just 3.6 miles from Memorial Hospital of Tampa, making it easy to access emergency care and other medical services. Tampa General Hospital and AdventHealth Carrollwood are also within a short drive.

NOVEL Beach Park is a convenient location for military personnel and their families. It is just 10.6 miles from MacDill Air Force Base and also offers easy access to other military bases, including the U.S. Coast Guard.
