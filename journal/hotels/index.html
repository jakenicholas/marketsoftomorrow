The Iconic Hotel List

A curated ranking of the hotels defining the modern travel experience — landmark properties, design-forward retreats, and the new openings setting tomorrow's standard.

— Hotels Curated by Markets of Tomorrow

The Iconic Hotels List is Markets of Tomorrow's curated record of properties that have left a lasting impression — shaped not by rankings or reputation, but by firsthand experience gathered over years of stays across cities, coastlines, and design eras.

Every hotel has been personally stayed in by our team, so each entry is grounded in a real, on-property perspective. From intimate independent inns to landmark grand hotels, the list spans a wide spectrum united by one standard: design integrity, service, setting, and a distinct sense of place.

"It's not about where to book, but about the hotels that stay with you long after checkout."

Our evaluations go beyond amenities. We weigh design — architecture, interiors, the intent behind every space — alongside the full experience: how the property flows from arrival to room, the rhythm of a stay, the lobby and the bar, the atmosphere that lingers after.

Rather than rigid scoring, the list is shaped by comparison, memory, and repeat stays. Hotels rise on how distinctly they stand apart and how strongly they resonate over time. Some earn their place through design, others through setting or history; the most iconic balance all three.
