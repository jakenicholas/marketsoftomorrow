What's happening in Miami residential right now

Miami's residential pipeline is dominated by Brickell supertalls, with 29 projects currently under construction and another 9 breaking ground this year. The cycle is led by Arquitectonica, which is the architect of record on 17 of the projects we track in the city — more than any other firm in any other US market.

The bulk of new product is branded residences — Cipriani, St. Regis, Four Seasons, Mercedes-Benz, Standard, Casa Tua — clustered along Brickell Avenue and around Biscayne Bay in Edgewater and Coconut Grove. Average tower height has climbed past 60 stories for the new cohort, and price-per-square-foot on top floors of the most-watched buildings has crossed $3,500 at contract.
